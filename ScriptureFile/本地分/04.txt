<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　彌勒菩薩說</span></span></span><br />
<a name="04p261"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　唐三藏沙門玄奘奉詔譯</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　韓清淨科記</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">本地分中有尋有伺等三地之一</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　本論以境行果的結構，分成十七地，說明由凡夫到成佛的境界。修行首先要認識目前生活在什麼境界，知道由什麼原因，會形成什麼結果，如何在因地上下工夫，這就是修行。修行之後，因緣具足，證得相應的聖道「果」。整部論的結構主要說明世間與出世間的因果，也是四諦、緣起的說明。前九地說的境界是三界緣起，說明欲界、色界、無色界種種顛倒的事情。接著說：〈聞所成地〉、〈思所成地〉、〈修所成地〉屬於三乘通行；還有〈聲聞地〉、〈獨覺地〉和〈菩薩地〉，屬於三乘別行，透過修行改善思想行為，使令自己的境界獲得改善及提升、出離三界乃至成就無上菩提。最後兩地：〈有餘依地〉和〈無餘依地〉，介紹果證；修行以後會有成果，依所修三乘觀行證得初果、二果、三果、四果，或辟支佛果，或無上菩提的佛果，可見境行果的結構安排得非常巧妙。前面的〈五識身相應地〉及〈意地〉，說明的是前六轉識的體相用，一切法以心為主，所以先說前六轉識。〈有尋有伺等三地〉說明由眾生不同的分別心變現出種種差別相，形成三界相，這是緣起法的粗相。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　科文分成二大科：第一科結前生後，結束前文生起後文。第二科總別標釋，總相標出及各別解釋，又分二科；第一科總標列，總相標示列舉出來，又分二科；第一科嗢柁南，以偈頌說明，第二科長行，用較長的文字解釋偈頌。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科廣分別；以五科詳細說明三界眾生的各種情況。第一科界施設建立；建立三界眾生的各種情況。界施設建立包括八種：一、數建立；說明什麼是界所攝，什麼不屬於界所攝，三界中哪些眾生屬於有尋有伺地？哪些是無尋唯伺地？哪些是無尋無伺地？二、處建立；說明三界眾生住處的建立。三、有情量建立；說明三界眾生的身量。四、有情壽建立；說明三界眾生的壽量。五、有情受用建立；說明三界眾生受用苦、樂、飲食、淫欲的詳細情況。六、生建立；說明三界有情受生的建立。七、自體建立；說明三界有情的自體建立。八、因緣果建立；說明三界眾生的因緣果報之建立。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科相施設建立，說明義相並以七相說明相施設建立。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科如理作意施設建立，以八相說明如理作意。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第四科不如理作意施設建立，以十六種外道論說明不如理作意。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第五科雜染施設建立，說明煩惱雜染、業雜染及生雜染。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丙三、有尋有伺等三地2　丁一、結前生後</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科〈有尋有伺等三地〉，說明三界眾生的各種情況，分二科；第一科結前生後，結束前面那一大段文，生起後面的一段文。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">已說意地。云何有尋有伺地？云何無尋唯伺地？云何無尋無伺地？</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　從卷1到卷3，已經將〈意地〉說過了。什麼是〈有尋有伺地〉？什麼是〈無尋唯伺地〉？什麼是〈無尋無伺地〉？「尋」是指粗的思惟觀察，「伺」是指細的思惟觀察。要略而言，心與尋伺欲相應稱為有尋有伺，唯與伺相應稱為無尋唯伺，不與尋伺欲相應稱為無尋無伺。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丁二、總別標釋2　戊一、總標列2　己一、嗢柁南</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科總別標釋，標出總相再各別解釋，總標是大概說，別釋是詳細地說，分二科；第一科總標列，總相標示列舉出來，又分二科；第一科嗢柁南，先用偈頌表達三界眾生的情況。&nbsp;</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">總嗢柁南曰：</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　界相如理不如理　雜染等起最為後</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　總頌是：界、相、如理、不如理；雜染等起為最後。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">己二、長行</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科長行，用較長的文字來解釋偈頌。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是三地，略以五門施設建立。一、界施設建立，二、相施設建立，三、如理作意施設建立，四、不如理作意施設建立，五、雜染等起施設建立。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　這三地就是三界，要略而言以五門來表示：一、界施設建立，二、相施設建立，三、如理作意施設建立，四、不如理作意施設建立，五、雜染等起施設建立。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p262"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">戊二、廣分別5　己一、界施設建立2　庚一、徵</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科廣分別，詳細分別，分五科；第一科界施設建立，說明三界的施設建立，又分二科；第一科徵，先問。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何界施設建立？</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　什麼是界施設建立？</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">庚二、釋2　辛一、總標列2　壬一、嗢柁南</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科釋，解釋，分二科；第一科總標列，總相標示列舉出來，又分二科；第一科嗢柁南，先用偈頌表達。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">別嗢柁南曰：</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　數處量壽受用生　自體因緣果分別</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　各別的偈頌是：數、處、量、壽、受用、生；自體、因、緣、果分別。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">壬二、長行</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科長行，用較長的文字解釋偈頌。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知界建立由八相。一、數建立，二、處建立，三、有情量建立，四、有情壽建立，五、有情受用建立，六、生建立，七、自體建立，八、因緣果建立。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　應當知道界建立有八相：</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一、數建立，屬於界的建立；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　二、處建立，三界眾生居住處所的建立；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　三、有情量建立，有情身量的建立；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　四、有情壽建立，有情壽量的建立；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　五、有情受用建立，有情受用苦、樂、受用淫欲、受用飲食的建立；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　六、生建立，有情受生的建立；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　七、自體建立，有情生命體形態的建立；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　八、因緣果建立，因緣果報的建立。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辛二、依次釋8　壬一、數建立2　癸一、徵</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科依次釋，依著次序解釋，分八科；第一科數建立，先說明三界數目的建立，又分二科；第一科徵，先問。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何數建立？</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　什麼是數建立？</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">癸二、釋3　子一、標列界</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科釋，解釋，分三科；第一科標列界，標列出三界。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">略有三界。謂欲界、色界、無色界。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　要略而言有三界。一切眾生的分別心不同，煩惱不同，歸納起來有三種，包括欲界、色界、無色界。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p263"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子二、辨攝別2　丑一、墮</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科辨攝別，說明屬於世間或出世間所攝，分二科；第一科墮，屬於三界所攝。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="3185:2542"></a>如是三種，名墮攝界。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　這三種屬於界所攝，名為墮攝界。欲界、色界、無色界有情，都還是生死凡夫，不是聖人，無法到涅槃那裡去，屬於世間法，所以稱為墮攝界。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》名墮攝界者：墮世間法，名墮所攝。世間有五。謂有情世間、器世間、欲世間、色世間、無色世間。如攝決擇分思所成地說。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/65.htm#2543:51918">陵本六十五卷十四頁5198</a>）今此但說三種世間，名墮攝界。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　墮世間法，稱為墮所攝。世間有五種：有情世間、器世間、欲世間、色世間、無色世間。如〈決擇‧思所成慧地〉卷65，2089頁所說。這裡只說三種世間，是指欲世間、色世間、無色世間，稱為墮攝界。有情世間是指能居之正報，指一切有情識的眾生，包括五趣輪迴的眾生都是，如：人、天、地獄、餓鬼、畜生。器世間是指自然界、無生物界、物質世界，山河、大地、草木、城宅等眾生可居住、生活之國土世界，以國土世界形如器物，能容受眾生，可變可壞，所以稱為器世間。此器世間相當於依、正二報中的依報。欲世間是指欲界的有情世間及器世間；色世間是指色界天的有情世間及器世間；無色世間是無色界天，無業果色，所以無器世間，唯指有情世間。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、非墮</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科非墮，不屬於三界所攝。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">非墮攝界者，謂方便，并薩迦耶滅，及無戲論無漏界。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　這一大段在說數建立，是眾生在三界裏的數量，用數目來分別，有三界：欲界、色界、無色界，如果用攝不攝別，是說落在世間或不落在世間，稱為辨攝別。墮就是落，墮攝界是指眾生墮在生死流轉所攝的三界，非墮攝界是指不屬於三界所攝，稱為非墮攝界。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　非墮攝界包括：</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一、方便，修習進入無漏界的道聖諦，如四念處等出世間道，修習聖道成就時，能出離生死，趣入非墮攝界；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　二、薩迦耶滅，指滅諦。薩是有之義，又含虛偽、移轉之義；迦耶，聚集之義，意譯作身。薩迦耶指偽身或壞聚，在五蘊身上執取有我稱為薩迦耶見。這個五蘊身無常敗壞、有老病死、是因緣所生的假法、沒有真實的體性，令人苦惱的，不是真的安樂住，可是愚痴無聞凡夫顛倒執著五蘊身是我或我所有的，因之起惑造業，數數在三界中取得果報，讓生命相續不斷。我見不滅，不能超出三界所攝，我見滅除就不再墮到三界流轉生死，所以薩迦耶滅稱為非墮攝。可見出離三界的重點就是要滅除我見；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　三、無戲論無漏界，無戲論是指無分別，無漏是指沒有煩惱流漏，這是聖人的境界，是指涅槃。聖人沒有煩惱的繫縛，自由自在，不墮就是不落。證入涅槃界，永斷三界愛見煩惱，遠離名言戲論分別，遠離煩惱的造作，不再流轉生死，是大解脫大安樂的境界。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》非墮攝界者等者：此中方便，謂道聖諦。薩迦耶滅，謂滅聖諦。涅槃界性，唯內所證，若異不異、死後當有或當無等一切戲論不能說故，是名無戲論無漏界。如是一切，不墮欲、色、無色三種世間，故名非墮攝界。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　這裡所說的方便，是指道聖諦。道，是指能成就聖人的道路，這件事是真實的，所以稱為諦。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　薩迦耶滅，是指滅聖諦，當我見滅除就可以證得聖道。滅，又指煩惱滅，有情滅除煩惱成為聖人，這是真實不虛的，所以也稱為諦，因此滅聖諦是非墮攝界。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　涅槃界是指無為法，不生不滅的無為境界唯是內心所證得、所體證的，離名言相、言語道斷、心行處滅。涅槃不可思議，如果修行成功證得涅槃時，那個境界是怎麼樣，是跟現在不一樣嗎？稱為若異；或是跟現在沒有什麼不同，稱為若不異。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　證入涅槃，或死後會有生命體嗎？或是沒有？這都是戲論。所有戲論都不能形容涅槃的境界，涅槃的境界不是名言分別所能達到的地方，也沒有煩惱的流漏，名為無戲論無漏界。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　如上所說方便：道聖諦；薩迦耶滅：滅聖諦；及無戲論無漏界：涅槃界；都不屬於欲、色、無色三種世間，稱為非墮攝界。&nbsp;</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子三、配屬地2　丑一、墮攝2　寅一、別辨相3　卯一、有尋有伺地</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科配屬地，各別解釋三界眾生，哪些配屬在〈有尋有伺地〉，哪些配屬在〈無尋唯伺地〉，哪些配屬在〈無尋無伺地〉，分二科；第一科墮攝，說明墮在三界眾生的情況，又分二科；第一科別辨相，各別說明其相狀，又分三科；第一科有尋有伺地，先說明什麼是〈有尋有伺地〉。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此中欲界及色界初靜慮，除靜慮中間若定、若生，名有尋有伺地。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　此指三界。三界中欲界是屬於〈有尋有伺地〉；色界有初禪二禪三禪四禪，色界的初禪天是屬於〈有尋有伺地〉；初禪要昇到二禪當中還有一個中間定，這中間定稱為靜慮中間，若定是指在欲界修定證得靜慮中間，若生是指證得靜慮中間定死後生到初禪天當大梵天王，靜慮中間若定、若生是屬於無尋唯伺地，所以初禪除去這個部分，靜慮中間以下及欲界都稱為〈有尋有伺地〉。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p264"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》此中欲界至名有尋有伺地者：欲有二種。一、煩惱欲，二、事欲。依此二種建立欲界。由此為因，起貪、恚、害尋，造身語意惡不善業，是故欲界名有尋有伺地。色界初靜慮中，由善尋伺對治欲界惡不善法，於常常時、於恆恆時，有尋有伺心行所緣躁擾而轉，不得寂靜，是故亦名有尋有伺地。謂定地中，於緣最初率爾而起怱務行境，麤意言性，是名為尋；即於彼緣隨彼而起、隨彼而行徐歷行境，細意言性，是名為伺。然於初靜慮中，若至上品善修習已，齊此說名靜慮中間。無尋相應，唯伺俱轉，依此建立無尋唯伺地。非此中說，故應除之。修習彼定，當生大梵眾同分中，是名靜慮中間若定、若生。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　色聲香味觸的欲有二種：</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一、煩惱欲，是由內心的分別所生的煩惱。對於事欲，隨逐愛味，依耽著識，發生種種虛妄分別貪，稱為煩惱欲。例如學習持午這條戒，雖然沒吃東西可是心裡一直在想吃，這就是煩惱欲。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　二、事欲，由外境所引生，有二種。一種、穀，穀所依處，指田事；二種、財，財所依處，如金銀等事。飲食等名事欲，此處以食物為例，其他的欲也是一樣。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　有時是內心想而已並沒有去做，那是煩惱欲；事欲是指由因緣所生、所愛味的色聲香味觸及金銀財食等。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　《俱舍論》的〈分別世品〉中說：「世間妙境非真欲，真欲謂人分別貪。」世間的妙境不是真的欲，真欲是人的分別心，那種貪心，使令自己覺得欲是好的。依此煩惱欲及事欲這二種，建立欲界。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　由此煩惱欲及事欲生起貪欲、瞋恚及想要傷害他人的尋思，進而造作身的惡不善業，語的惡不善業，意的惡不善業，所以欲界名〈有尋有伺地〉。貪欲這件事情使令有情落在欲界，初禪沒有欲，為什麼也稱為〈有尋有伺地〉？因為色界初禪依尋伺趣入離欲，在修禪定時要對治欲界的欲，經常要對欲的愛味、過患、出離，作粗的及細的尋伺，所以也稱為〈有尋有伺地〉。然而色界初禪的有尋有伺與欲界的有尋有伺不同。初禪的有尋有伺沒有欲、恚、害的尋思，只是心仍不能離尋伺，不能入深定離開有尋有伺地。&nbsp;</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　尋，指生在初禪天的人或欲界有情修初禪時，於離欲的所緣境上最初一剎那接觸境界，第六意識依止名言在所緣境上匆匆忙忙作粗略的觀察，稱為尋。尋伺主要是第六意識的功能。在離欲的所緣境上，如不淨觀等的觀法中，隨順粗的思惟觀察之後進一步生起細的思惟觀察，接著持續觀察，慢慢的經歷，慢慢思惟離欲的所緣境。第六意識依止名言在所緣境上作微細的觀察，稱為伺。伺是更詳細的思惟觀察。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　初禪的定力有上品、中品、下品的差別，定的品質比較差的屬於下品，尋伺比較頻繁；中品的定，尋伺少一點，因為不須很用力就可以離欲了；上品的定是已經達到上品善修，離欲已經修得很好，不用很費力就離欲。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　已經快要將尋伺捨棄接近二禪時，稱為靜慮中間，這時沒有粗的思惟觀察，只有細的思惟觀察，此時不與尋心所相應，唯與伺心所相應，稱為〈無尋唯伺地〉。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　初禪到二禪的中間定稱為〈無尋唯伺地〉。〈無尋唯伺地〉不是這裏的〈有尋有伺地〉所說的範圍，所以應該將其除去。因此前面那一段文說除靜慮中間。如果修習初禪到二禪的中間定，將來會到大梵天的眾同分中受生，當大梵天王，稱為靜慮中間若定、若生。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　欲界的人為了得到欲，生起貪、恚、害尋思而造業，這在歷史的故事中有很多例子，貪欲的結果總是令人受苦。通常不是為了江山，就是為了女人、大家互相爭鬥殘殺。所以欲界的人因為要得到欲，生起貪、恚、害尋思而造業，稱為有尋有伺地。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　總之〈有尋有伺地〉是指初禪除上品定力相應的靜慮中間以下及欲界的眾生，因為這些人與尋伺心所相應思惟欲或思惟離欲。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、無尋唯伺地</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科無尋唯伺地，說明無尋唯伺的範圍。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">即靜慮中間若定、若生，名無尋唯伺地。隨一有情，由修此故，得為大梵。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　依欲界身修初禪到二禪中間的定、或生到初禪當大梵天王，稱為〈無尋唯伺地〉。此時細的思惟分別離欲所緣，就可以離欲了，境界較高。如果有情能修得初禪到二禪的中間定，將來就可以生為大梵天王。經典上有人問佛陀說：佛教裏面教那麼多人修禪定，那麼多人要當大梵天王有地方去嗎？佛陀說：世間有很多三千大千世界，很多地方可以當大梵天王，不用擔心這件事情。所以盡管修，不用擔心沒地方去。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、無尋無伺地</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科無尋無伺地，說明無尋無伺的範圍，〈無尋無伺地〉完全沒有尋伺欲。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">從第二靜慮，餘有色界及無色界全，名無尋無伺地。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　從二禪以上，定力已經穩定，不用再依尋伺而修離欲，因此第二靜慮以上乃至非想非非想處都稱為〈無尋無伺地〉。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p265"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》從第二靜慮至無尋無伺地者：第二靜慮以上，於諸尋伺能見過失，尋伺麤相皆無所有。依此建立無尋無伺地。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　從二禪以上，對於種種的尋伺能看見使心躁動的過失，尋伺的麤相沒有了，由此二禪以上的人建立無尋無伺地，因為沒有粗的，也沒有細的離欲尋伺。於三界眾生依於有或無尋伺欲而建立〈有尋有伺地〉、〈無尋唯伺地〉、〈無尋無伺地〉。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、隨難釋2　卯一、標簡</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科隨難釋，隨著解釋比較困難的地方，分二科；第一科標簡，標示簡別無尋無伺的道理。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="44580:2558"></a><a name="27170:2558"></a>此中由離尋伺欲道理故，說名無尋無伺地，不由不現行故。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　二禪以上的有情，因為依止已經離尋伺欲的道理，因此稱為〈無尋無伺地〉，不是沒有尋伺的現行，只要有第六意識都可以尋伺。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、釋因2　辰一、由一類無</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科釋因，解釋原因，分二科；第一科由一類無，有一種未離欲界欲者，於一時間也有無尋無伺意現行。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">所以者何？未離欲界欲者，由教導作意差別故，於一時間亦有無尋無伺意現行。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　為什麼這樣說？因為沒有離欲界欲的人，如果遇到善知識教導不淨觀，也會如理作意，於一時間剎那趣入定的境界也有無尋無伺作意現行。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、由一類有</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科由一類有，有一種已離尋伺欲者，也有尋伺現行。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">已離尋伺欲者，亦有尋伺現行。如出彼定及生彼者。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　已經離尋伺欲的有情，也有尋伺現行。如欲界能入二禪以上諸定的有情，從二禪以上諸定出定以後，也會有尋伺現行；生到二禪天以上的有情意識還在，也有尋伺現行，可是沒有尋伺欲，還是稱為〈無尋無伺地〉。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p266"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》此中由離尋伺欲道理故至如出彼定及生彼者者：此釋無尋無伺地建立所以。有尋伺貪，名尋伺欲。不相應起，是名為離。由離彼欲，是故建立無尋無伺地；非由尋伺不現行故，說名無尋無伺地。依此道理，有尋有伺地、無尋唯伺地皆應準知。由諸異生欣樂喜樂略有多種。謂欣樂欲生喜樂、欣樂有尋有伺定生喜樂、欣樂無尋有伺定生喜樂、欣樂無尋無伺定生喜樂差別故。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/70.htm#2564:54889">陵本七十卷八頁5473</a>）於煩惱欲未相應離，及於事欲未境界離，是名未離欲界欲者。彼聞通達修瑜伽師殷勤教誨，於修作意如應安立正修行時，由於六想作意思惟，心於所緣安住一境。即於爾時尋伺不行，然不說名無尋無伺地。何以故？未離尋伺欲故。云何六想？謂無相想、無分別想、寂靜想、無作用想、無所思慕無躁動想、離諸煩惱寂滅樂想。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/32.htm#2564:26390">陵本三十二卷五頁2624</a>）又有欣樂無尋無伺補特伽羅，是名已離尋伺欲者。雖於定地尋伺不行，然出定時及生彼地，亦有尋伺現行。不由現行說名有尋有伺地。何以故？已離尋伺欲故。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　這是解釋建立〈無尋無伺地〉的道理。內心有尋伺貪，也就是有啟動尋伺心所的欲求，稱為尋伺欲。反之，不與此尋伺貪相應，稱為離。由遠離彼尋伺欲，因此建立〈無尋無伺地〉；並不是由於尋伺不現行，而稱為〈無尋無伺地〉，而是由於沒有尋伺欲。依止這個道理，以有沒有尋伺欲作為標準、作為界限，〈有尋有伺地〉、〈無尋唯伺地〉都應準照這種道理可知。異生是指凡夫，凡夫是不同的，思想不同，身體不同，有種種的不同，因為每個人欣喜好樂的境界不一樣，有些眾生喜歡欲，看到欲會很喜歡而且生起喜樂的心情；有些歡喜初禪的有尋有伺定；有些喜歡初禪到二禪的中間定，想做大梵天王；有些喜歡二禪天以上屬於無尋無伺的喜樂乃至捨等境界。如〈決擇‧聲聞地〉卷70，2183頁所說。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　於煩惱欲的相應沒有遠離，於色等事欲的境界沒有遠離，稱為未離欲界欲者。由於聽聞通達修三乘觀行的瑜伽師殷切精勤的教導訓誨，在修各種作意及於所應安立的法門正確修行時，由於六種想作意思惟，心於所緣安住在同一個境界，就在此時尋伺不現行，但不能稱為〈無尋無伺地〉，為什麼？因為還沒有離尋伺欲的緣故。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　什麼是六想？指無相想：於所緣離色等一切相；無分別想：於能緣的心離一切分別想；寂靜想：於滅涅槃，起寂靜想；無作用想：於法生無實作用想；無所思慕無躁動想：無所思惟愛慕無躁動無散亂之想；離諸燒惱寂滅樂想：遠離愛見煩惱生寂滅樂想。如〈聲聞地〉卷32，1045頁所說。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又有欣樂無尋無伺的有情，稱為已離尋伺欲者。雖然於定地尋伺不現行，然而出定時及生到〈無尋無伺地〉，也有尋伺現行。因此不是由尋伺現行，而安立為〈有尋有伺地〉；而是由尋伺欲，而安立為〈有尋有伺地〉。為什麼？因為〈無尋無伺地〉這一類的有情已經遠離尋伺欲故。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、非墮攝2　寅一、明有尋伺2　卯一、標</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科非墮攝，說明不屬於三界，屬於無漏界，無為或無漏有為的境界，分二科；第一科明有尋伺，說明有尋伺，又分二科；第一科標，先標出來。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若無漏界有為定所攝初靜慮，亦名有尋有伺地。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　無漏界指沒有煩惱，聖人的境界。若無漏界依於修習有為的禪定所攝的初禪而證得，也稱為〈有尋有伺地〉。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、釋</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科釋，解釋。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="55378:2568"></a>依尋伺處法，緣真如為境入此定故，不由分別現行故。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　依著尋伺心所，以初禪為依，緣真如為境而入無分別智，入無漏定以後就不分別，不會再尋伺。這不是由於分別尋思現行，才稱為〈有尋有伺地〉，而是依初禪中有尋伺法，依尋伺的方法入定，因此稱為〈有尋有伺地〉。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p267"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》若無漏界至分別現行故者：此釋尋伺義通無漏。若依初靜慮，以分別所攝如理作意思惟真如，即此初靜慮名無漏界有為定所攝。當知此依初靜慮中有尋伺法，緣真如為境而入此定；是故說名有尋有伺地。不由分別現行而立此名。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　這是解釋尋伺的道理，也可通於無漏。以初禪為依修無漏來說，就是尋伺真如。如果依止初禪，以第六意識的分別心所攝的如理作意思惟真如，則此初禪即稱為無漏界的有為定所攝。應當知道這是依初禪不離尋伺法，緣著真如為所緣境而入此定；因此稱為有尋有伺地。不是由尋伺現行而安立這種名稱。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、指說所餘</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科指說所餘，指出其他的如前所說。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">餘如前說。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　其餘道理，如前所說，無漏界也通於無尋唯伺、無尋無伺。若依中間定入無分別無戲論無漏界，則屬〈無尋唯伺地〉，若依二禪以上除非想非非想處定而入無分別無戲論無漏界，則屬〈無尋無伺地〉。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》餘如前說者：謂如前說，非墮攝界者，謂方便，及薩迦耶滅，及無戲論無漏界。當知無漏遍一切地。無尋唯伺、無尋無伺，說之為餘。此不具說，故指如前。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　如前所說，不屬於三界所攝界，指道諦的方便，滅諦的薩迦耶滅，及無戲論無漏界的涅槃界。應當知道無漏界遍於有尋有伺等三地。無尋唯伺、無尋無伺，稱為餘。這裏沒有完全說出來，因此指出如前所說。聖人的心可以在有尋有伺等三地裏面活動。以上是用數建立，說明三界的眾生屬於有漏界或無漏界，有漏界分成三地，無漏界也可以分成三地。有漏界依有沒有尋伺欲來界定三地，無漏界是依於所依禪定不同來界定三地的差別。&nbsp;</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">壬二、處所建立3　癸一、欲界3　子一、辨所立2　丑一、總標</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科處所建立，說明三界眾生處所的建立，分三科；第一科欲界，先說明欲界的情況，又分三科；第一科辨所立，說明所安立的處所，又分二科；第一科總標，總相標出有三十六處。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">處所建立者，於欲界中有三十六處。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又處所建立方面，於欲界中有三十六個處所。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、別列4　寅一、那落迦處2　卯一、辨二種2　辰一、大那落迦2　巳一、標列名</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科別列，各別列出三十六處，分四科；第一科那落迦處，又名地獄，又名無可救度，又分二科；第一科辨二種，辨明二種地獄，又分二科；第一科大那落迦，先說大地獄，又分二科；第一科標列名，標列出八大地獄的名稱。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂八大那落迦。何等為八？一、等活，二、黑繩，三、眾合，四、號叫，五、大號叫，六、燒熱，七、極燒熱，八、無間。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　在欲界有八個大地獄：是哪八個大地獄呢？一、等活地獄；二、黑繩地獄，用繩子綁住；三、眾合地獄，四、號叫地獄，叫得很慘的；五、大號叫地獄，更慘的；六、燒熱地獄，渾身被燒的；七、極燒熱地獄，八、無間地獄。這是屬於八個大那落迦。那落迦在中國翻譯成地獄，根據《大毗婆沙論》卷172解釋。那落是造義，迦是惡義，造惡之者生彼處故，還有很多種意思。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p268"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、釋廣量</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科釋廣量，解釋大地獄的空間有多大。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此諸大那落迦處，廣十千踰繕那。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一踰繕那又名一由旬是十六華里，十千是一萬，一萬乘以十六華里是十六萬華里。這些大地獄處，寬達一萬由旬，合十六萬華里。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、寒那落迦3　巳一、標</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科寒那落迦，說明寒冰地獄，分三科；第一科標，標出有八種寒冰地獄。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此外復有八寒那落迦處。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　除此之外，又有八種寒冰地獄。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、徵</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科徵，問。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">何等為八？</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　是哪八種寒冰地獄？</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、列</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科列，列出八種寒冰地獄。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">一、皰那落迦，二、皰裂那落迦，三、喝哳詀那落迦，四、郝郝凡那落迦，五、虎虎凡那落迦，六、青蓮那落迦，七、紅蓮那落迦，八、大紅蓮那落迦。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一、皰那落迦，二、皰裂那落迦，三、喝哳詀那落迦，四、郝郝凡那落迦，五、虎虎凡那落迦，以上五種約受苦眾生呼出來的聲音而安立；六、青蓮那落迦，受苦眾生皮膚像青蓮花一樣的地獄；七、紅蓮那落迦，受苦眾生皮膚像紅蓮花那樣的地獄；八、大紅蓮那落迦，受苦眾生皮膚像大紅蓮那樣的地獄。以上三種依受苦眾生皮膚皸裂的樣子而安立。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、明邊際2　辰一、舉大那落迦2　巳一、等活</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科明邊際，說明大那落迦及寒那落迦的邊際，分二科；第一科舉大那落迦，舉大地獄為例，又分二科；第一科等活，先舉出等活大那落迦。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">從此下三萬二千踰繕那，至等活那落迦。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　從此欲界往下三萬二千個十六華里處，有一個地獄名等活那落迦。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、所餘</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科所餘，其他的地獄。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p269"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">從此復隔四千踰繕那，有餘那落迦。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　在等活那落迦下的深層，每隔四千踰繕那就有另外一個那落迦。第一個是等活，再往下是黑繩，再往下是眾合，每個地獄之間又隔四千踰繕那，就是四千個十六華里，那麼樣的深度，有其餘的那落迦。所以地獄是一直往下，可知在地獄下還有地獄。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、例寒那落迦2　巳一、初寒那落迦</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科例寒那落迦，說明寒冰地獄的處所，分二科；第一科初寒那落迦，第一個寒冰地獄的處所。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如等活大那落迦處，初寒那落迦處亦爾。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　如等活地獄處，第一個寒冰地獄也一樣，從欲界往下三萬二千個十六華里處。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、餘寒那落迦</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科餘寒那落迦，其餘寒冰地獄的處所。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">從此復隔二千踰繕那，有餘那落迦應知。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　寒冰地獄之間的距離是兩千踰繕那。從第一個寒冰地獄次第往下兩千踰繕那，有其餘的寒冰地獄。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、餓鬼處</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科餓鬼處，說明餓鬼的處所。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又有餓鬼處所。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又有餓鬼所住的處所。這裏沒有詳細說，《大毘婆沙論》中說餓鬼的住處有二種：</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一是「正住處」，在閻浮提五百由旬下面，被閻羅王統領，也是在地下，不過沒那麼深。又《善生優婆塞經》，也同此說。五百由旬之下有閻羅鬼王城，周匝四面七萬五百千由旬，王領鬼眾於中止住。又如《五道苦經》說：此之餓鬼，正住彼鐵圍兩山中間。二是「邊住處」，也有二種。一有威德，二無威德。有威德的餓鬼，住山谷、空中或住海邊，都有宮殿，果報過人。沒有威德者，或依不淨糞穢而住，或依草木塚墓而止，或依屏廁或舊房子而居，都沒有房舍屋宅可住，果報比人下劣。詳細情形如《法苑珠林》卷6所說可知。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、非天處</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科非天處，阿修羅所住的處所。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又有非天處所。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又有非天的阿修羅所居住的處所。非天是指阿修羅，男的很醜，女的很美。《楞嚴經》卷9說到阿修羅有卵生、胎生、濕生、化生四種。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　卵生的阿修羅，因為擁護佛法的功德力，乘著神通升入天空，屬鬼道所攝。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　胎生的阿修羅是在天道中，因為沒有天德被貶墜，住在靠近日月的地方，屬於人趣所攝。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　濕生的阿修羅，是住在大海下面，福德劣，住在水穴口，日間遊行於虛空，夜間歸宿於水底，因濕氣而生，屬於畜生道所攝。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　化生的阿修羅，能掌握世界，勢力很大，而且沒有畏懼，能夠跟大梵天王和帝釋天、四天王天爭權，有天福沒有天德，屬於天趣所攝。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅四、人天處2　卯一、攝傍生趣</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第四科人天處，說明人與天居住的地方，分二科；第一科攝傍生趣，人與天住的地方有畜生在其中，但是畜生住在哪裡並沒有特別安立出來。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">傍生即與人天同處，故不別建立。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　傍生是指畜生，畜生是遍在諸趣都有。人住的地方有畜生，像：貓、狗、蟑螂、螞蟻等，天上也有，如鳥等，所以沒有將畜生另外建立一個住的處所。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、辨人天趣2　辰一、人2　巳一、四大洲</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科辨人天趣，說明人及天住的地方，分二科；第一科人，說明人居住的地方，又分二科；第一科四大洲，列出四大洲。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有四大洲，如前說。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又有四大洲，如前面所說，有：南贍部洲，東毗提訶洲，西瞿陀尼洲，北拘盧洲。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p270"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、八中洲</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科八中洲，也是人住的地方。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有八中洲。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又有八中洲，這也是人類居住的地方。八中洲是指在四大洲的每一個洲旁邊都會有二個比較小的陸地，稱為八中洲。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　如《瑜伽論記》說，東毗提訶洲的二個洲，稱為提訶洲或毘提訶洲；南贍部洲的二個洲，稱為遮末羅和筏羅遮末羅；西瞿陀尼洲的二個洲，稱為捨諦和嗢怛羅曼怛里拏；北拘盧洲的二個洲，是矩拉婆和憍拉婆。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、天2　巳一、總標列</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科天，說明天人的住處，分二科；第一科總標列，總相標示列出有六處。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又欲界天有六處。一、四大王眾天，二、三十三天，三、時分天，四、知足天，五、樂化天，六、他化自在天。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又欲界天有六個處所：</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一、四大王眾天，居住在持雙山，比須彌山要低一半；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　二、三十三天，住在須彌山的頂端，帝釋天住在此處；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　三、時分天，是指夜摩天，這是空居天，住在須彌山上升虛空中四萬由旬處；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　四、知足天，在夜摩天之上，稱為兜率天，是彌勒菩薩住的地方；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　五、樂化天，自己有神通力能變化欲塵自己享樂；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　六、他化自在天，能由自己或由樂化天變化出欲塵而受用欲樂。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、明所攝</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科明所攝，說明魔宮為他化自在天所攝。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="24815:2610"></a>復有摩羅天宮，即他化自在天攝，然處所高勝。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又有魔王的天宮，屬於他化自在天所攝，可是他住的地方更高、更殊勝。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子二、攝其餘2　丑一、舉種類2　寅一、那落迦攝</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科攝其餘，說明其他處所，分二科；第一科舉種類，舉出屬於哪一類所攝，又分二科；第一科那落迦攝，獨一那落迦及近邊那落迦，屬於二種大地獄所攝。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有獨一那落迦、近邊那落迦，即大那落迦及寒那落迦。以近邊故，不別立處。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又有獨一那落迦，這是依各人罪業所招感的地獄，孤散於虛空或曠野之地獄，不像八寒、八熱地獄有決定的處所；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　近邊那落迦，指近大地獄及寒冰地獄旁一百二十八個遊增地獄。因為靠近大那落迦與寒那落迦的旁邊附近，所以沒有各別安立另外一個處所。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、人中攝</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科人中攝，人中也有獨一那落迦。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又於人中，亦有一分獨一那落迦可得。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又在人所住的處所中，也有一部分的獨一地獄可得。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、引經說</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科引經說，引出經中所說為證。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p271"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如尊者取菉豆子說：我見諸有情，燒然、極燒然、遍極燒然，總一燒然聚。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　如尊者取菉豆子，即大目犍連，說：我看到諸有情，燒然，被火所燒；極燒然，被火燒得很厲害；遍極燒然，整個身體都燒到了；總一燒然聚，燒成一團火。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　經典上說，當一群人到郊外去時，有人走到一個地方就變成一團火，他人都看不到，只有那人認為那是地獄，於是就墮到那曠野的地方，受獨一那落迦的苦報。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　在人所住的四大洲中，除北俱盧洲以外，其餘三洲都有近邊那落迦及獨一那落迦。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子三、總結數</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科總結數，總結有三十六處。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是等三十六處，總名欲界。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　如前所說眾生居住的三十六個處所，總稱為欲界。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》如是等三十六處總名欲界者：謂八大那落迦處、八寒那落迦處、餓鬼處、非天處、四大洲、八中洲、六欲天，是名欲界三十六處。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　包括八大地獄、八寒地獄、餓鬼處、非天處、四大洲、八中洲、六欲天，稱為欲界三十六處。欲界有這三十六個處所。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">癸二、色界2　子一、總標</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科色界，說明色界眾生居住的處所，分二科；第一科總標，總相標出有十八個處所。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，色界有十八處。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　其次，色界有十八個處所。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》色界有十八處者：謂梵眾天、梵前益天、大梵天、少光天、無量光天、極淨光天、少淨天、無量淨天、遍淨天、無雲天、福生天、廣果天。復有無煩天、無熱天、善現天、善見天、色究竟天。復有超過五淨居地大自在住處。如是十八處，總名為色界。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　梵眾天、梵前益天、大梵天，這三天是初禪天下中上三品的名稱。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　初禪天由下往上；最下一層稱為梵眾天，屬於百姓；第二層梵前益天也名梵輔天，屬於大臣；第三層大梵天是國王，大梵天王住的地方。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　二禪天由下往上分別是：少光天、無量光天、極淨光天是。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　三禪天由下往上分別是：少淨天、無量淨天、遍淨天。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　四禪天有：無雲天、福生天、廣果天，凡夫居住的有這三天。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又有無煩天、無熱天、善現天、善見天、色究竟天的五淨居宮，這是三果以上聖人住的處所。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又有超過五淨居地大自在住處。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　本論說，十地菩薩是在大自在天上成佛，在人間成佛的菩薩是化身，那裏才是報身成佛處。這十八處，總稱色界。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子二、別列4　丑一、初靜慮攝</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科別列，各別列舉出來，分四科；第一科初靜慮攝，初禪所攝梵眾天等三天。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="9616:2625"></a>謂梵眾天、梵前益天、大梵天，此三由軟中上品熏修初靜慮故。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　梵眾天、梵前益天、大梵天，這三天由熏修初禪時定力有軟中上品差別。下品的生到梵眾天，中品的生到梵前益天，上品的生到大梵天。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p272"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、第二靜慮攝</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科第二靜慮攝，二禪所攝少光天等三天。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">少光天、無量光天、極淨光天，此三由軟中上品熏修第二靜慮故。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　少光天、無量光天、極淨光天，這三天由熏修二禪時定力有軟中上品差別，下品的生到少光天、中品的生到無量光天、上品的生到極淨光天。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑三、第三靜慮攝</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科第三靜慮攝，三禪所攝少淨天等三天。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">少淨天、無量淨天、遍淨天，此三由軟中上品熏修第三靜慮故。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　少淨天、無量淨天、遍淨天，這三天由熏修三禪時定力有軟中上品差別，下品的生到少淨天、中品的生到無量淨天、上品的生到遍淨天。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑四、第四靜慮攝3　寅一、異生2　卯一、顯生處</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第四科第四靜慮攝，四禪攝無雲天等九天，分三科；第一科異生，凡夫住的天，又分二科；第一科顯生處，顯示凡夫住處。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">無雲天、福生天、廣果天，此三由軟中上品熏修第四靜慮故。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　無雲天、福生天、廣果天，這三天由熏修四禪時定力有軟中上品差別，下品的生到無雲天、中品的生到福生天、上品的生到廣果天。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、攝無想</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科攝無想，無想天也是在第四禪，屬「廣果天」所攝。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">無想天即廣果攝，無別處所。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　無想天是廣果天所攝，在廣果天裏另外有一個無想天，沒有安立另外一個處所。無想天是修無想定所感的異熟果報。生此天者，念想滅盡，僅存色身及不相應行蘊，因此稱無想天。這是外道婆羅門的最高涅槃處，也是異生凡夫以「出離想」作意，修無想定，所感得的異熟果報。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、諸聖2　卯一、顯生處2　辰一、標</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科諸聖，說明聖人住的天，分二科；第一科顯生處，顯示聖人的住處，又分二科；第一科標，標出五淨居天。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="9611:2636"></a>復有諸聖，住止不共五淨宮地。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又有三果以上聖人，住在四禪三天之上五種清淨的宮殿，不共於色界四禪的凡夫而住。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、列</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科列，列出五淨居天。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂無煩、無熱、善現、善見，及色究竟。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一、無煩天，此天離欲界苦及色界樂，苦樂兩滅，心與境不交接，沒有煩惱，名為無煩天。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　二、無熱天，此天通達此心與境交接之念，無依無處，所以無熱惱，清涼自在，名無熱天。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　三、善見天，一心在定，定能發慧，於慧心中，見十方界圓遍澄凝，更無世界內外塵象，及無心昏沉之垢，因此名為善見天。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　四、善現天，住此天的聖者，其果德易於顯現，名善現天。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　五、色究竟天，此天究竟諸色幾微之處，入無邊際，為色界天最勝之天。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　據《俱舍論》說，這五天次第而上。據《楞嚴經》說，這五天，橫在第四禪天中，但四禪天的凡夫天，只能聞這五天之名，而不能知見。如世間聖地道場，多有羅漢所居，而凡人不能見。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、辨修因</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科辨修因，說明生到淨居天的因緣。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p273"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由軟、中、上、上勝、上極品雜熏修第四靜慮故。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　三果以上的聖人修四禪時，為什麼會跟凡夫不一樣呢，因為有「雜熏修」。《俱舍論》卷24載，由雜修五品，可感生於五淨居。五品指雜修第四靜慮之五品，即：</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一、下品，謂雜修第四靜慮者，初起多念無漏，次起多念有漏，後復起多念無漏，如此旋還，後又漸減，到三心現前，第一心是無漏，第二心有漏，第三心是無漏，便得靜慮成備；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　二、中品，即六心現前；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　三、上品，即九心現前；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　四、上勝品，即十二心現前；</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　五、上極品，即十五心現前。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　初（下）品三心現前便得成滿，是唯從一念無漏，引起一念有漏現前，無間復生一念無漏。第二（中）品六心現前，是指再從多念無漏相續現前，引生多念有漏，後復多念無漏現前。乃至二念無漏，次引二念有漏現前，無間復生二念無漏。次後唯從一念無漏，引起一念有漏現前，無間復生一念無漏。到此又一個雜修定根本圓成，三心現前成滿。加上初品三心共成六心。如此乃至五次來回練磨至上極品，共十五心現前。由此五品雜修靜慮如其次第感五淨居。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、菩薩2　卯一、顯生處</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科菩薩，說明菩薩的住處，分二科；第一科顯生處，顯示菩薩居住的地方。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有超過淨宮大自在住處。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又有超過五淨居宮的大自在天住的地方，是菩薩的住處。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、辨修因</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科辨修因，說明生到大自在天的因緣。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">有十地菩薩，由極熏修第十地故，得生其中。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　有十地菩薩，由極熏修第十地的功德，而得生於大自在天宮。以上說明色界有情的住處。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">癸三、無色界</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科無色界，說明無色界有情的住處。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，無色界有四處所，或無處所。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　其次，無色界有四個處所，或沒有處所。依《中華佛教百科全書》說：此界由於無色，故無法定其方處。但以業因有勝劣，故果報亦有勝劣之差別。由於果報之差異，乃使此界細分為四類，稱為四無色，或四無色天、四無色處。即︰</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一、空無邊處天︰由修空無邊處定所感得之果報，該定由厭棄色界一切色相，觀想虛空無邊而入。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　二、識無邊處天︰由修識無邊處定而感得之果報，該定由厭棄虛空無邊，故捨虛空，轉心緣識，依觀想識無邊而入。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　三、無所有處天︰由修無所有處定而感得之果報，該定由厭棄識之無邊，故捨緣識，轉心依無所有法而入。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　四、非想非非想處天︰由修該定感得之果報，該定非如色界及前述三定之勝想，故稱非想，亦非如無想滅盡定之非想，故稱非非想。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　前三者從所修入定之因行而得名，後者則從果相而立名。關於無色之義，眾說紛紜，如大眾部、化地部即認為「無色」應是無粗顯之色，而非無細微之色，若全然無色，則於無色界命終而轉生於欲、色二界時，色從何得生﹖說一切有部以為，既言無色，即蘊含「色已全無」之意，因此，若說細微之色為無色，則水中細蟲或色界之身，豈非亦可稱為無色！又，三界生死相續之狀，或色續無色，或無色續色，故不可一概說諸色斷已。經部認為往昔所起之異熟因，熏習在心，其功能今已成熟，故色法仍能生起。至於大乘之意，則認為業果之色雖已不存在，但定果之色仍存。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》無色界有四處所等者：謂空無邊處、識無邊處、無所有處、非想非非想處，如是四處名無色界。或由無色，名無處所。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　無色界有四種處所，是指空無邊處、識無邊處、無所有處、非想非非想處，這四種處所稱為無色界。或由無色，稱為無處所。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">壬三、有情量建立2　癸一、辨有色界4　子一、人2　丑一、贍部洲</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科有情量建立，建立三界有情的身高數量，分二科；第一科辨有色界，說明欲界及色界有情的身高數量，又分四科；第一科人，說明人的身高數量，又分二科；第一科贍部洲，南贍部洲人身量不定。 （連結：<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/C04-01.htm">三界有情身量、壽量對照表</a>）</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">有情量建立者，謂贍部洲人身量不定，或時高大，或時卑小，然隨自肘三肘半量。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　有情身量建立方面，南贍部洲的人身量不一定，劫初時人很少，可是很高大，到人壽只有十歲的時候身量很小，隨自己手肘的三倍半高。我們現在所居住的地方也是有很高大的，兩百公分的都有，也有很矮小的幾十公分的也有。肘是印度長度的單位，橫列二十四指為一肘，因為南贍部洲人身量不定，或時高大，或時卑小，所以每一個人的手指長度不同，以自己手指為準，二十四指的長度乘於三倍半大約就是個人的身高。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、餘三洲2　寅一、東毗提訶</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科餘三洲，其他三洲有情的身量，分二科；第一科東毗提訶，說明東毗提訶人的身量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p274"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">東毗提訶身量決定，亦隨自肘三肘半量，身又高大。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　東毗提訶人身高都是一樣，也是隨自肘三肘半量。身又高大，身體比南贍部洲的人還高大。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、西瞿陀尼等</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科西瞿陀尼等，西瞿陀尼等身量也是隨自肘三肘半量，更加高大。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如東毗提訶如是，西瞿陀尼、北拘盧洲身量亦爾，轉復高大。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　如東毗提訶身量隨自肘三肘半量，如是西瞿陀尼洲隨自肘三肘半量，北拘盧洲人身量也隨自肘三肘半量，又更高大。綜合而言，最矮小的是南贍部洲人，其次是東毗提訶洲，其次是西瞿陀尼洲，北拘盧洲人身量最高大。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子二、天2　丑一、欲界2　寅一、舉初四天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科天，說明天人的身量，分二科；第一科欲界，欲界天人的身量，又分二科；第一科舉初四天，舉四天王天眾等的身量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">四大王眾天身量如拘盧舍四分之一。三十三天身量復增一足。帝釋身量半拘盧舍。時分天身量亦半拘盧舍。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　四天王天眾的身量是拘盧舍的四分之一。拘盧舍，是二華里，二華里的四分之一等於半里。三十三天比四天王還要高，住在須彌山頂上，又增一足。將一個拘盧舍分成四分，再將四分之一除以四就是一足。三十三天的身量是拘盧舍的四分之一再加上一個拘盧舍的十六分之一。這是一種算法。第二種算法是，將拘盧舍分成四分，然後再分成二分。就是四分之一拘盧舍再加上一個拘盧舍的八分之一。所以可能是0.625或0.75華里，這是三十三天的身量。古代的里跟現代的里不一定相同，如果以一尺三十公分來計算，一里十尺，300公分，二里600公分的四分之一是150公分，也是很矮，所以不能按照現在的里來算。帝釋身量半拘盧舍，就是一華里，一定比人高很多。時分天（夜摩天）身量也是半拘盧舍。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、例所餘天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科例所餘天，所餘天人的身量各增一足，越往上身量越高。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此上一切，如欲界天身量，當知漸漸各增一足。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　時分天以上一切諸天，如欲界天的身量，應當知道漸漸各增一足的高度，越往上的天人身高越高。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、色界3　寅一、舉初四天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科色界，色界天人的身量，分三科；第一科舉初四天，舉初四天的身量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">梵眾天身量半踰繕那。梵前益天身量一踰繕那。大梵天身量一踰繕那半。少光天身量二踰繕那。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一個踰繕那是十六華里，半踰繕那，是八華里那麼高。梵眾天身量半踰繕那，比欲界天的天人還要高很多。梵前益天身量一踰繕那，是十六華里。大梵天身量一踰繕那半，是二十四華里。少光天身量二踰繕那，是三十二華里。越往上越高大。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、例所餘天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科例所餘天，所餘天人的身量各漸倍增。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此上一切餘天身量各漸倍增。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　從少光天以上諸天，身量各漸加倍增長。如少光天身量是二踰繕那、光音天身量是四踰繕那，無量光天身量是八個踰繕那，少淨天身量是十六個踰繕那，遍淨天身量是三十二個踰繕那，無量淨天身量是六十四個踰繕那。這樣一直往上，各各倍數漸增。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p275"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、簡無雲天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科簡無雲天，簡別無雲天。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">除無雲天。應知彼天減三踰繕那。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　如果這樣一直算上去，無雲天的身高應該是一百二十八踰繕那，這裡說減三踰繕那，剩下一百二十五個踰繕那。一百二十五個踰繕那再乘以16華里，是2000華里，非常的高大。福生天身量是二百五十踰繕那，廣果天身量是五百踰繕那。無想天和廣果天在一起，所以也是五百踰繕那。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子三、那落迦等2　丑一、舉大那落迦2　寅一、標</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科那落伽等，地獄眾生的身量也是很高大，分二科；第一科舉大那落迦，舉大地獄裏的眾生身量，又分二科；第一科標，標出大地獄的眾生身量不定。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又大那落迦身量不定。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又大地獄的眾生身量不一定。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、釋</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科釋，解釋。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若作及增長極重惡不善業者，彼感身形其量廣大；餘則不爾。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　若造作惡業以後沒有後悔心還生起歡喜心，使令惡業增長，這種惡不善法的身語意三業，墮到地獄時，大地獄眾生的身形其量廣大。為什麼身體很廣大？因為受苦的面積比較多。其他沒有作及增長極重惡的人，身量就不會像大地獄眾生那樣廣大了。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、例寒那落迦等</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科例寒那落迦等，寒冰地獄等有情身量與大地獄也是一樣。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如大那落迦如是，寒那落迦、獨一那落迦、近邊那落迦、傍生、餓鬼亦爾。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　如大地獄是這樣，寒冰地獄，獨一地獄，近邊地獄、畜生、餓鬼也是一樣。如果造作及增長極重的惡業，則身形廣大，受的苦更大，其他就沒那麼大。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子四、非天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第四科非天，說明阿修羅的身量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">諸非天身量大小，如三十三天。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　前面說過四大王眾天身量如拘盧舍四分之一。三十三天身量又增一足。所以阿修羅的身量也是很高大，像三十三天那麼高大，就是0.75或0.625華里。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">癸二、簡無色界</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科簡無色界，簡別無色界有情沒有身量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知無色界無有色故，無有身量。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　應當知道無色界天的人因為沒有色法，所以沒有身高的問題。以上說明三界眾生的身量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">壬四、壽建立2　癸一、別辨壽量3　子一、欲界6　丑一、人4　寅一、南贍部洲</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第四科壽建立，說明三界眾生壽命的長短，分二科；第一科別辨壽量，各別說明壽命的長短，又分三科；第一科欲界，欲界眾生的壽命長短，又分六科；第一科人，人的壽命長短，又分四科；第一科南贍部洲，南贍部洲人的壽量不定。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p276"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="30366:2679"></a>壽建立者，謂贍部洲人壽量不定。彼人以三十日夜為一月，十二月為一歲。或於一時壽無量歲，或於一時壽八萬歲，或於一時壽量漸減乃至十歲。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　壽命的建立方面，南贍部洲的人壽命不一定。那裡的人以三十個日間及夜間為一個月，十二個月是一年。有時是無量歲，就是劫初時；或於一時壽八萬歲，就是到了住劫之初；或於一時壽量漸減乃至十歲，就是減劫時，壽量漸漸減到十歲。所以人的壽量是不一定的。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、東毗提訶</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科東毗提訶，東毗提訶人的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">東毗提訶人壽量決定二百五十歲。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　東勝神洲的人壽量決定是二百五十歲。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、西瞿陀尼</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科西瞿陀尼，西瞿陀尼人的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">西瞿陀尼人壽量決定五百歲。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　西瞿陀尼洲的人壽量是決定五百歲。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅四、北拘盧洲</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第四科北拘盧洲，北拘盧洲人的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">北拘盧洲人壽量決定千歲。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　北拘盧洲的人壽量決定是一千歲。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、天3　寅一、四大王眾天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科天，天人的壽量，分三科；第一科四大王眾天，說明四大王眾天人的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又人間五十歲是四大王眾天一日一夜。以此日夜，三十日夜為一月，十二月為一歲。彼諸天眾壽量五百歲。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又人間五十年是四大王眾天的一日一夜。以這種日夜來算四大王眾天的壽命，三十天是一個月，十二月為一年。他們的壽命是五百歲。換算成人間的壽命就是九百萬歲，壽命非常長。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、三十三天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科三十三天，三十三天壽量更長，是四大王眾天的二倍。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">人間百歲是三十三天一日一夜。以此日夜如前說，彼諸天眾壽量千歲。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　人間一百年是三十三天一日一夜。以這種日夜來算，三十天是一個月，十二月為一年。他們的壽命是一千歲，這是天上的時間。如果以人間的時間來算，是三千六百萬歲。</span></span></span></div>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　有一個故事說有一個服侍帝釋天的宮女，這宮女死了以後到人間來受生成為女人。她知道自己前一生是天上的人，心想趕快回到天上，擔心帝釋天會來找她。怎麼樣可以回到天上呢？她想了一個辦法，就是很努力的布施、供養佛陀及出家人，供養完後，到五十歲時就死掉了，死後又生到三十三天去。當她回到天上以後，天王問：我怎麼半日沒看到你，你去哪裡了？她說：我到人間去受生投胎。這裏說人間百歲，剛好天上一日一夜，她活了五十歲，又回到天上去，如果是天上的時間就是半日。這是說三十三天的壽命是這麼長。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p277"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、所餘諸天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科所餘諸天，所餘諸天壽量就更多了。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是所餘，乃至他化自在天，日夜及壽量各增前一倍。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　如是其餘諸天，乃至他化自在天，日夜及壽量各比前一天增長一倍。如果用天上的時間算，三十三天是一千歲，時分天（夜摩天）是二千歲，兜率天是四千歲，化樂天是八千歲，他化自在天是一萬六千歲。他們的壽命越往上越長。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑三、八大那落迦4　寅一、等活</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科八大那落迦，八大地獄眾生的壽量，分四科；第一科等活，等活地獄眾生的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又四大王眾天滿足壽量是等活大那落迦一日一夜，即以此三十日夜為一月，十二月為一歲。彼大那落迦壽五百歲。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　滿足壽量是指沒有中夭來說。又四大王眾天壽量滿五百歲是等活大那落迦一日一夜，以此單位三十日夜為一月，十二月為一年。這大那落迦壽量五百歲，是四大王眾天的好幾倍。所以地獄有情受的苦非常長，而越往下活得越久，活得越久受苦越久。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、黑繩等</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科黑繩等，黑繩地獄眾生的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如以四大王眾天壽量成等活大那落迦壽量，如是以三十三天壽量成黑繩大那落迦壽量，以時分天壽量成眾合大那落迦壽量，以知足天壽量成號叫大那落迦壽量，以樂化天壽量成大號叫大那落迦壽量，以他化自在天壽量成燒熱大那落迦壽量，應知亦爾。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　如以四大王眾天壽量成等活大那落迦壽量，如是三十三天的壽量一千歲，是黑繩大那落迦的一日一夜，三十天為一個月，十二個月為一年，這樣黑繩大那落迦的壽命是一千歲，這樣計算地獄眾生的壽命真的是太長了。以時分天壽量二千歲是眾合大那落迦一日一夜，三十天為一個月，十二個月為一年，大那落迦的壽命是二千歲。以知足天壽量四千歲是號叫大那落迦一日一夜，三十天為一個月，十二個月為一年，號叫大那落迦壽量四千歲。以樂化天壽量八千歲是大號叫大那落迦一日一夜，三十天為一個月，十二個月為一年，大號叫大那落迦壽量八千歲。以他化自在天壽量一萬六千歲，是燒熱大那落迦一日一夜，三十天為一個月，十二個月為一年，燒熱大那落迦壽量是一千六百歲。道理都是一樣，應當了知。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、極燒熱</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科極燒熱，極燒熱地獄的眾生壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">極燒熱大那落迦有情壽半中劫。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　極燒熱大地獄眾生壽量是半中劫。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅四、無間</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第四科無間，無間地獄眾生的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p278"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">無間大那落迦有情壽一中劫。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　無間大地獄壽量是一中劫。一個減劫一個增劫成一中劫，時間非常長，越往下壽命越長。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑四、非天等</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第四科非天等，阿修羅等的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">非天壽量如三十三天。傍生、餓鬼壽量不定。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　非天的壽命像三十三天一樣一千歲。畜生餓鬼的壽命就不決定有多長了。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑五、八寒那落迦</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第五科八寒那落迦，說明八寒地獄眾生的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又寒那落迦於大那落迦次第相望，壽量近半應知。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又八寒地獄相對於八大大地獄的次第來說，壽命是大地獄有情的一半，也是很長的，應當了知。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑六、近邊等那落迦</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第六科近邊等那落迦，說明近邊地獄眾生的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又近邊那落迦、獨一那落迦受生有情壽量不定。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又靠近八大地獄及八寒地獄的近邊地獄，獨一地獄的有情壽命就不一定。以上說明欲界眾生的壽命。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子二、色界3　丑一、舉初四天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科色界，色界眾生的壽量更長，越往高的地方壽命就越長，分三科；第一科舉初四天，舉初四天眾生的壽量為例。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">梵眾天壽二十中劫一劫，梵前益天壽四十中劫一劫，大梵天壽六十中劫一劫，少光天壽八十中劫二劫。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　一個成住壞空是八十中劫，梵眾天的壽命是二十中劫為一劫。梵前益天壽命是四十中劫為一劫。大梵天的壽命是六十中劫為一劫。成住壞空要六十中劫才全部破壞掉燒到初禪天，所以初禪天最高是六十中劫。少光天是二禪天最低一層天，壽命二大劫，一個成住壞空有一大劫，等於二個成住壞空。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、例所餘天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科例所餘天，所餘天人的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">自此以上，餘色界天壽量相望，各漸倍增。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　少光天以上，其於色界諸天的壽量相望，各漸加倍增長。如少光天以八十中劫為一劫，壽命是二劫，無量光天是四劫，極淨光天是八劫。他們的壽量都是用劫來算的，八十中劫為一劫，往上就是四劫、八劫、十六劫、三十二劫、六十四劫等。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑三、簡無雲天</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科簡無雲天，無雲天壽減三劫。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p279"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">唯除無雲。當知彼天壽減三劫。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　這樣往上倍增，唯除無雲天的壽量是一百二十八劫減掉三劫，為一百二十五劫。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子三、無色界</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科無色界，無色界眾生的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">空無邊處壽二萬劫，識無邊處壽四萬劫，無所有處壽六萬劫，非想非非想處壽八萬劫。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　空無邊處壽量二萬劫，識無邊處壽量四萬劫，無所有處壽量六萬劫，非想非非想處壽量八萬劫。三界最高的壽量是八萬劫。經典上說：寧做提婆達多墮地獄，也不生到非想非非想處天。這是因為提婆達多墮地獄去這件事很慘，至少還有佛法可以熏習，只是他熏習錯、做錯事。非想非非想處天壽命八萬劫，這麼長的時間都沒辦法聽到佛法、修學佛法，所以不願意生到非想非非想處天。壽量八萬劫，太久了，在那裏定深慧淺，佛教不主張到那裏去受生，這也是八處障難之一。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">癸二、料簡差別2　子一、有中夭無中夭</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科料簡差別，料理思量簡別壽量的差別，分二科；第一科有中夭無中夭，說明有夭折及沒有夭折的情況。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">除北拘盧洲，餘一切處悉有中夭。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　除了北拘盧洲的人決定一千歲，沒有中夭，其餘一切處都有中夭。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子二、有滓身無滓身</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科有滓身無滓身，指有沒有屍體的差別。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又人、鬼、傍生趣有餘滓身；天及那落迦與識俱沒，無餘滓身。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　渣滓是指屍體。又人、鬼和畜生死的時候會有屍體，天和地獄，因為是化生的，死時與阿賴耶識一起消失，沒有剩餘的遺體。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》人鬼傍生趣有餘滓身等者：於四生中，卵胎濕生既命終已，有餘滓身。化生不爾，無餘滓身。此中且約人、鬼、傍生一分非化生者，說有滓身。天及那落迦全，唯是化生，是故說彼無餘滓身。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　在四種生命體當中，卵生、胎生、濕生的眾生死後，都有屍體。化生有情不是這樣，死後沒有剩餘的身體。這是約人、鬼、畜生一分不是化生，而說有屍體。天人和所有的地獄眾生都是化生，沒有屍體，不用處理屍體的問題。以上是說明三界有情的壽量。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">壬五、受用建立2　癸一、總標列</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第五科受用建立，建立三界有情的受用差別，分二科；第一科總標列，總相標出受用的差別。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">受用建立者，略有三種。謂受用苦樂、受用飲食、受用婬欲。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　受用建立方面，要略而言有三種。包括受用苦樂、受用飲食、及受用淫欲。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p280"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">癸二、隨別釋3　子一、受用苦樂2　丑一、辨差別2　寅一、受用苦3　卯一、略辨相</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科隨別釋，接著各別解釋，分三科；第一科受用苦樂，說明受用苦樂的情形，又分二科；第一科辨差別，說明受用苦樂的差別，又分二科；第一科受用苦，說明受用苦的情況，又分三科；第一科略辨相，簡單說明受用苦的相狀。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="40512:2726"></a>受用苦樂者，謂那落迦有情多分受用極治罰苦；傍生有情多分受用相食噉苦；餓鬼有情多分受用極饑渴苦；人趣有情多分受用匱乏追求種種之苦；天趣有情多分受用衰惱墜沒之苦。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　受用苦樂方面，地獄的有情多分受用被極端對治處罰的痛苦；畜生多分受用互相吞噉的痛苦，如常說的食物鏈，老虎吃小的動物，小的動物又吃更小的動物。餓鬼眾生多分受用非常飢餓、口渴的痛苦；在人的果報裏面多分受用缺乏與追求種種名聞利養的痛苦，這裡不是說全部，有些人也沒有。天趣有情多分受用衰惱墜沒的痛苦。諸天最苦的果報是受用衰惱，五衰相現時，不樂本座、花也枯了、身體有臭味、流汗等，即將死亡時的苦，是天人最大的苦。以上說明欲界有情受苦差別。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、廣分別5　辰一、那落迦有情4　巳一、八大那落迦8　午一、等活那落迦3　未一、標</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科廣分別，詳細說明受用苦的有情，分五科；第一科那落迦有情，地獄的有情，又分四科；第一科八大那落迦，八大那落迦又名八熱地獄，又分八科；第一科等活那落迦，說明等活地獄的苦，又分三科；第一科標，標出受極治罰苦。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又於等活大那落迦中，多受如是極治罰苦。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　又於等活大地獄中，多分受用下文所說的極端對治處罰的痛苦。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、釋3　申一、顯苦因</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科釋，解釋，分三科；第一科顯苦因，顯示受苦的原因。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂彼有情，多共聚集，業增上生，種種苦具次第而起，更相殘害，悶絕躄地。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　等活地獄的有情，多半是聚在一起，由於罪業特別的強，種種的苦具按次第現出苦惱的境界，彼此之間更互相殘害，而悶絕暈倒橫躺在地面上。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、辨苦緣</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第二科辨苦緣，說明等活地獄有情感受痛苦的因緣。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">次虛空中有大聲發，唱如是言：此諸有情可還等活！可還等活！次彼有情欻然復起，復由如前所說苦具更相殘害。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　其次在虛空中發出廣大的聲音說：這些有情可以繼續回到等活地獄！可以繼續回到等活地獄！這些有情本來暈倒在地上，聽到聲音忽然又活起來，又拿起苦具互相殘害。死了又活起來，稱為等活。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申三、明邊際</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科明邊際，說明受苦的邊際。</span></span></span></div>

<p><a name="04p281"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由此因緣，長時受苦，乃至先世所造一切惡不善業未盡未出。</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　由於這個因緣，長時間受互相殘害的苦，乃至過去生所造的惡業，業力還沒有完全盡，還不能出離這個地方以前，都一直要受這種苦。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未三、結</span></strong></span></span></p>

<div class="cb" style="margin-bottom:4px; margin-left:16px; margin-top:4px; text-align:start"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#002200"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　第三科結，結說名等活地獄。</span></span></span></div>

<p><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">故此那落迦</span></strong></span></span></p>
