<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　彌勒菩薩說</span></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7327"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　唐三藏沙門玄奘奉詔譯</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　韓清淨科記</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">攝事分中契經事菩提分法擇攝第四之二</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">癸四、第四嗢柁南2　子一、頌標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，嗢柁南曰：</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　邪師住雪山　勸勉繫屬淨　漸次戒圓滿　穗成就為後</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子二、長行釋8　丑一、邪師住2　寅一、明專自利2　卯一、標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">有諸外道，於弟子眾自立為師，專求利養、專求恭敬、專求自利。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、釋2　辰一、舉二言2　巳一、外道師</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7328"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">遇緣和合，有族姓子投其出家。因而謂曰：汝之與我先無一切資身眾具可共受用，汝應為我往詣他處，褒讚我德，掩藏我失，我亦為汝行如是事。我等二人迭相依護，當於諸王若與王等乃至一切大商主邊，多獲利養及以恭敬。若作是言，諸外道師名專自利。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、彼弟子</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">然其弟子便發抗言：勿為此見！如是護者，未名自護往惡趣失；若防此失，乃名自護。是故汝應如前自護，我亦當自別為餘護。我既不能護汝，汝亦不須護我。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》如前自護者：謂如前說，防惡趣失，乃名自護應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、辨德失</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於此義中，當知弟子是如理語者，是聰慧者，重當來故。應知其師是非理語者，是愚癡者，重現在故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、明不護失2　卯一、標失</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有雜染觸惱於他，由雜染故，不能自護；因此惱他，不名護他。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、釋義2　辰一、當自護</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7329"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此中如前由親近等斷諸煩惱，名當自護。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》如前由親近等斷諸煩惱者：此說如前，亦謂自護。防惡趣失，唯斷煩惱。當知此由親近善士、聽聞正法、如理作意、法隨法行為其因緣。如是正行，名當自護。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、當護他2　巳一、標名</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">從此已後，由斷為因，不惱他等，名當護他。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、釋義2　午一、由無瞋無害</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">應知此中，無瞋無害，是無惱義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、由一切哀愍2　未一、正顯此義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">無緣而起利樂二心，無緣而起慈悲二心，當知如此是哀愍義。由哀愍故，不惱於他。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、出彼相違</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">是故當知，一切哀愍與彼相違。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7330"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、雪山2　寅一、喻善解脫2　卯一、標列三地</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，應知雪山喻佛善說法毗奈耶，此中略有三分可得。一、無學地，二、有學地，三、異生地。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、配釋其相3　辰一、於無學地</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">猿猴喻彼非理作意諸相應心，獵人喻魔，於無學地俱不能行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、於有學地</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於有學地乃至不還，唯有非理作意相應猿猴喻心獨一能往，非獵人喻魔所能行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰三、於異生地</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於異生地二俱能行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、出不解脫</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又諸愚夫，要觀餘境能出餘境，追求餘境餘境所縛，是故於境不得解脫。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑三、勸勉2　寅一、出四義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7331"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，由於正法聽聞、受持、觀察義理、法隨法行，如其次第，應知勸化安立四義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》應知勸化安立四義者：勸謂勸導，化謂調伏，安謂安處，立謂建立。菩薩自性利行有此四別，如攝事品說應知。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/43.htm#74352:34207">陵本四十三卷十三頁3520</a>）如是四義，如其次第，配屬聽聞、受持、觀察義理、法隨法行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、廣三力3　卯一、標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有三法，尚能斷除一切勝妙婬欲貪纏，況乎鄙劣諸欲貪纏。何等為三？一、精進力，二、不放逸力，三、對治力。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、配釋</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由精進力，其已生者令不堅住。由餘二力，其未生者令不得生。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、結顯</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是行者勤修正行，為欲斷除已生惡故，及未生者令不生故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑四、繫屬2　寅一、指修相</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7332"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，於四念住殷重修習，如聲聞地應知其相。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、明繫屬2　卯一、約三界辨2　辰一、繫屬魔</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">繫屬魔者，謂在欲界，此不還果即能超度。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、繫屬死</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">繫屬死者，謂從欲界乃至有頂，此阿羅漢乃能超度。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、約有情辨2　辰一、出差別3　巳一、不清淨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">言不清淨諸有情者，謂諸異生。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、清淨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">言清淨者，謂諸有學。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、鮮白</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">言鮮白者，謂諸無學。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、廣清淨2　巳一、出四證淨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有四種證淨，未清淨者能令清淨，已清淨者能令鮮白。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7333"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、辨其分位</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知此中，上諸有學說名清淨，下諸有學名不清淨，彼由修道未清淨故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》當知此中上諸有學等者：謂於四證淨中，諸有學位上下有別，依此說名清淨及不清淨。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">餘如前說。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》餘如前說者：鮮白名餘。唯是無學，名如前說。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑五、淨漸次2　寅一、標列五種</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，修四念住，應知略有五種漸次。一、信增上力，清淨出家；二、戒律儀；三、根律儀；四、樂遠離；五、蓋清淨。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、簡非在家</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">諸在家者，雖復數數修諸念住，獲得淨信，諸蓋清淨，然闕學處，當知所修不得圓滿。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑六、戒圓滿4　寅一、標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7334"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，由三因緣，具戒苾芻當知禁戒淨命圓滿。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、徵</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何為三？</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">一、所行圓滿，二、攝取圓滿，三、受用圓滿。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅四、釋3　卯一、所行圓滿</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">所行圓滿者，謂從買賣，乃至害、縛、斷截、撾打、揣摩等事，皆悉遠離。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、攝取圓滿</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">攝取圓滿者，謂於攝取象馬等事，乃至攝取生穀等事，皆悉遠離。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、受用圓滿</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">受用圓滿者，謂衣僅蔽身，食纔充腹，便生喜足。於餘長物、非時食等，皆悉遠離。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑七、穗2　寅一、標差別</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7335"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，身等四法如四大路，於彼所生非理作意，如邪祈願稻穀麥穗；於彼所生如理作意，如正祈願稻穀麥穗。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、明有漏</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知欲界是不定地，猶如其皮；色無色界俱是定地，猶如其肉；無明如血。於三界中，由三種漏有淋漏義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》當知欲界是不定地等者：此中義顯，於三界中身等四法，為三種漏之所淋漏。謂在欲界，為彼欲漏之所淋漏，猶如其皮。色無色界，為彼有漏之所淋漏，猶如其肉。通一切界，為無明漏之所淋漏，猶如其血。內外麤細，義有差別，故作是喻。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑八、成就3　寅一、總標簡</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，如先所說所有貪等種種無量惡不善法，由二因緣若成就者，不能修習四種念住，非是一切泛成就者。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》非是一切泛成就者：義顯泛成所餘因緣，容能修習四種念住故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7336"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、別列相</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何為二？一、有貪等纏現前故；二、於此纏不見過故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、釋所以2　卯一、由雜染心</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">纏現在前雜染心故，不能修習。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於此二種善巧，苾芻如實了達，正知而住。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7364"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、隨難釋2　卯一、舉不調善</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由諸作意有加行故，精進太過；又由前後有增減故，運轉不等。由此二緣，當知名為道不調善。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、翻名調善</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">與此相違，二因緣故，名道調善。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、喻自在2　寅一、總標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如轉輪王，於四洲渚得大自在，所獲七寶；如是心王，於四聖諦得大自在，所獲真淨七覺支寶，當知亦爾。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、別辨7　卯一、喻念覺支</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂於奢摩他、毗鉢舍那雙品運轉，降伏一切煩惱勝怨。由此義故，初念覺支猶如輪寶。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、喻擇法覺支</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">所知境相其量無邊，能知智體亦隨廣大。由此義故，擇法覺支猶如象寶。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7365"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、喻精進覺支</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">依此速能，乃至往彼所行、所得殊異勝處。由此義故，精進覺支猶如馬寶。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯四、喻喜覺支</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">悅意無罪，最為殊勝。由此義故，其喜覺支猶如女寶。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯五、喻輕安覺支</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">身心映徹，有所堪能。由此義故，輕安覺支如神珠寶。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯六、喻定覺支</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">能辦一切所欣求事。由此義故，其定覺支如藏臣寶。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯七、喻捨覺支</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">能摧一切染汙法軍，能率一切清淨法軍，能趣無相安隱住處。由此義故，其捨覺支如軍將寶。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子六、住2　丑一、標喻</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7366"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，諸修行者得七覺支，譬如大王有妙衣篋，三時受用。三分安住彼七覺支，當知亦爾。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、釋相3　寅一、三時</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">言三時者，謂初日分時、中日分時、後日分時。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、三分</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">言三分者，謂奢摩他品、毗鉢舍那品及其俱品。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、安住3　卯一、支具闕</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於初分中住四覺支，第二分中住四覺支，第三分中具足安住七種覺支。諸修行者，未曾安住唯一覺支。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、無怨等</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又七覺支，於諸外道無怨憎故，無違競故，恆懷利益意樂轉故，一切煩惱皆離繫故，說名無怨、無敵、無害、無有災患。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、如實知</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7367"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若修行者，於七覺分隨時現前、隨量現前，說名為住。若時退出，說名為滅。於是一切如實了知。彼由如是正知住故，名無罪住，無有愛味，心離味染。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子七、修3　丑一、標列因緣</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》二十一種想俱行等者：聲聞地中說有十想。一、不淨想，二、無常想，三、無常苦想，四、苦無我想，五、厭逆食想，六、一切世間不可樂想，七、光明想，八、離欲想，九、滅想，十、死想。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/28.htm#74732:23354">陵本二十八卷五頁2341</a>）今不淨想別開為九，謂青瘀想乃至空想。所餘想中，除離欲想及與死想，依三三摩地總攝為三，名無願行想，及空行想、無相行想。即此三想，并前十想總攝別開，合共建立二十一想。修諸覺支與此二十一想俱行，或俱時有，是名相應；或次第起，是名無間。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<a name="98p7368"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">無常等想俱行修，乃至死想俱行修者，據相應義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、據無間義2　卯一、舉不淨等</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">不淨等想俱行修，乃至觀空想俱行修者，據無間義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、例修慈等</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">慈等俱行修，應知亦爾。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》慈等俱行修等者：謂諸聖者，修習四無量定與覺支俱，是名慈等俱行修。應知此亦據無間義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑三、廣明對治2　寅一、修無常等及不淨等2　卯一、標列所治</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又於過去、未來、現在一切行中，諸行愛染，若懶惰懈怠，若薩迦耶見雖已斷滅習氣隨縛我慢現行，若貪味愛，若於世間種種妙事欲樂貪愛，若有所餘煩惱隨眠，若希求利養，若希求活命，若諸欲愛，若諸有愛，若隨虛妄分別所起四種欲貪。一、美色貪，二、形貌貪，三、細觸貪，四、承事貪。如是能令生起所有非理過患，及令其心越路而轉。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">對治彼故，隨其所應，有二十一想俱行修覺支差別。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、配釋修想6　辰一、修無願行想</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、修空行想</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">為欲對治一種障故，修空行想，苦無我想。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》為欲對治一種障故等者：此一種障，謂即前說薩迦耶見。修苦無我想能正對治。此即空行想攝。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰三、修無相行想</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">為欲斷滅所餘煩惱隨眠障故，修於三界無相行想。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》修於三界無相行想者：謂於斷界、離欲界、滅界觀見最勝寂靜功德，修習斷想、離欲想、滅想故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰四、於諸欲中修過患想</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">為欲對治希求利養及欲愛故，於諸欲中修過患想。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰五、修習死想</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　初內外力　清淨差別　異門沙門　婆羅門後</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">癸二、長行釋4　子一、內外力2　丑一、標列最勝</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》其四大種可令變異等者：謂四大種可令諸聖弟子變異有苦，非聖弟子可令四大有變異故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子三、天路2　丑一、出無惱害</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7401"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，若第一義清淨諸天，說名最勝無有惱害，由身語意畢竟無有惱害事故。即依如是清淨天性，說四證淨名為天路。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》若第一義清淨諸天者：前說有二淨天，一、不善淨天，二、善淨天。若唯能入世俗定者，當知是天不善清淨，於諸諦中不了達故，其心未得善解脫故。若能證入內法定者，當知是天名善清淨，於諸諦中已了達故，其心已得善解脫故。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/87.htm#75081:66270">陵本八十七卷三頁6561</a>）今此唯取善清淨天，是故說彼名第一義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、明修隨念2　寅一、總標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又四證淨為所依止，諸聖弟子，依三種門修六隨念。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、別辨3　卯一、辨所為</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">一者、為斷奢摩他品諸隨煩惱所起染惱；二者、為斷毗鉢舍那品諸隨煩惱所起染惱；三者、為斷雖無染惱，而於未來當可生起二隨煩惱。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、辨隨惑</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7402"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知此中，惛沈睡眠，名奢摩他品諸隨煩惱。欣樂諸欲俱行掉舉、貪等過失所生不善欲尋伺等，令心流散諸雜染法，名毗鉢舍那品諸隨煩惱。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、辨差別</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又由勝義諦理所得隨念，名義威勇。由世俗諦理所得隨念，名法威勇。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子四、喻明鏡</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，譬如有人執持明鏡，為觀自面淨不淨相；如是如來諸聖弟子，執持微妙證淨明鏡，為如實觀自身所有染淨諸相。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子五、記別2　丑一、果證差別2　寅一、辨前三果3　卯一、預流</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，若有成就四種證淨，唯即依自四種證淨為他記別，不依上位能順歡喜所修隨念。由此因緣，當知記別預流果證，未趣上位所修道故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、一來2　辰一、標因緣</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若於上位能順歡喜五種隨念，為他記別。由是因緣，當知記別一來果證。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7403"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》上位能順歡喜五種隨念者：修所成地說：入聖諦現觀已，為欲證得速疾通慧，作意思惟諸歡喜事。謂聖弟子，已見聖諦，已得證淨。即以證淨為依止故，於佛法僧勝功德田，作意思惟發生歡喜。又依自增上生事，及決定勝事，謂己身財寶所證盛事，作意思惟發生歡喜。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/20.htm#75098:17605">陵本二十卷二十三頁1795</a>）此中有五隨念，謂佛隨念、法隨念、僧隨念、戒隨念、捨隨念，如次應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、釋所由</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由三摩地未成滿故，於離欲道未圓滿故，於彼諸天未現見故。為求離欲，修習能順歡喜諸法；由此歡喜為所依故，發生輕安；由輕安故，領受身樂；由受樂故，心得正定，而於靜定未得成滿。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、不還</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若於上位六種隨念，為他記別。由是因緣，當知記別不還果證。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="98p7404"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》若於上位六種隨念等者：前五隨念及天隨念，名六隨念。由於上位已能現見諸天眾故，是故增天隨念為六隨念。顯揚論說：隨念記別，謂不還果，已得根本定，現見諸天眾，與梵眾等共興言論，隨念所求自乘功德未圓滿故。（顯揚論二十卷二頁）其義應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、簡最上果</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">阿羅漢果唯出世道乃能趣證，所有隨念唯是世間，是故不還果證已上，更無如是隨念記別。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、證淨差別</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又四證淨，預流果中唯說為淨，於餘學果說圓滿淨，於最上果說為第一圓滿清淨。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">戊四、結指餘</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是略引隨順此論境智相應諸經宗要摩怛理迦，其餘一切隨此方隅，皆當覺了。</span></strong></span></span><br />
