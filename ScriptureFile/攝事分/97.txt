<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　韓清淨科記</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">攝事分中契經事菩提分法擇攝第四之一</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">己四、菩提分法擇攝2　庚一、結前生後</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是已說緣起、食、諦、界擇攝。菩提分法擇攝，我今當說。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">庚二、標釋一切2　辛一、總嗢柁南</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">總嗢柁南曰：</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　念住與正斷　神足及根力　覺道支息念　學證淨為後</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辛二、別嗢柁南9　壬一、念住攝4　癸一、初嗢柁南2　子一、頌標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">別嗢柁南曰：</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　沙門沙門義　喜樂一切法　梵行數取趣　超二染為後</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7254"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子二、長行釋8　丑一、沙門2　寅一、總標簡</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">依四念住修習增上，由四因緣，應知內法有沙門道，及有究竟；外法決定無沙門道，亦無究竟。當知他論，諸沙門道及以究竟，一切皆空。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、別釋相2　卯一、略釋一切4　辰一、四種因緣</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名為四種因緣？一者、依止四處，得四證智故；二者、解脫四種外隨煩惱故；三者、內法弟子與外道弟子不同品類故；四者、內法大師與外道師不同品類故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、內法沙門4　巳一、徵</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名為內法沙門？</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂諸沙門略有四種。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">一者、勝道沙門，二者、論道沙門，三者、命道沙門，四者、汙道沙門。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7255"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳四、指</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">是四沙門若略若廣，如聲聞地已辯其相。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》如聲聞地已辯其相者：聲聞地說：當知諸善逝，名勝道沙門；諸說正法者，名說道沙門；諸修善行者，名活道沙門；諸行邪行者，名壞道沙門。乃至廣說應知。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/29.htm#73629:24660">陵本二十九卷十七頁2461</a>）</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰三、內法道2　巳一、徵</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">內法道者，云何為道？</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、釋2　午一、出體性</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂八支聖道。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、明施設2　未一、唯內法有</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若處施設八支聖道，是處施設汙道為後四種沙門。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、簡外法無</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7256"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若有其道，自行邪行，非生道器；由是因緣，容有汙道。是故外法尚無汙道，況得有餘。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰四、內法究竟</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">內法究竟者，云何究竟？謂斷諸取。諸取斷已，當來畢竟無復相續。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、廣釋因緣4　辰一、第一因緣攝2　巳一、徵</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名為依止四處？云何復名得四證智？</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、釋2　午一、四依處</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂四處者，一、三結永斷蘇息處，二、無退墮法勢力處，三、定趣菩提種類處，四、極七反有隨行處。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、四證智</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">依此四處，於佛法僧及於淨戒得證淨智。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、第二因緣攝3　巳一、徵</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名為解脫四種外隨煩惱？</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7257"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">一者、解脫現法外隨煩惱；二者、解脫後法外隨煩惱；三者、解脫展轉互相違戾所作外隨煩惱；四者、解脫於諸聖諦不能宣說、不能覺悟所作外隨煩惱。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、釋2　午一、辨煩惱4　未一、第一現法外隨煩惱</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知此中，諸外道類闕念住故，其念忘失，不正知住，領納諸受或樂、或苦、或非苦樂，於樂起染，於苦起恚，於非苦樂發起愚癡。如是名為第一現法外隨煩惱。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、第二後法外隨煩惱</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼由如是染恚癡故，以受為緣，生後有愛；以愛為緣，發生諸取；有愛、取故，以取為緣，成辦於有；廣說乃至純大苦聚積集增長。如是名為第二後法外隨煩惱。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未三、第三外隨煩惱</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又諸外道，薩迦耶見以為根本種種見趣，意各別故，彼此展轉互相違戾。是名第三外隨煩惱。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7258"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未四、第四外隨煩惱</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又諸外道，遍於一切四聖諦中，尚無有能施設其教，況當覺悟。是故彼於自師宗智雖得增上，而實無知，墮無明趣。是名第四外隨煩惱。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、明解脫</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">住內法者，於是一切皆能解脫。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰三、第三因緣攝2　巳一、徵</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何內法弟子與外道弟子不同品類？</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、釋2　午一、外道弟子</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂外道弟子，或墮有見常邊，或墮無見斷邊，長夜積集，深起藏護。由聞親近，由思染著，由修染著。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》長夜積集深起藏護者：攝異門分說：一切愚夫異生，於其六處，由執我故，名藏；執我所故，名護。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/84.htm#73667:64366">陵本八十四卷十一頁6384</a>）此說藏護，應準彼知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7259"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、內法弟子</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">內法弟子行處中行，遠離二邊。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰四、第四因緣攝3　巳一、徵</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何內法大師與外道師不同品類？</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、釋2　午一、外道師3　未一、標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="67808:73674"></a>謂外道師，於一切取雖同宣說斷遍知論，而於諸取不能施設正斷遍知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、釋2　申一、由未全斷4　酉一、能於欲取立斷遍知</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由彼本契出家捨欲，故於欲取立斷遍知，非於自見、自戒、我語。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">酉二、於分見取立斷遍知</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若有與他諸餘沙門、婆羅門等，見不同分，戒禁同分，彼於見取亦能隨分立斷遍知，非於戒禁、我語二取。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">酉三、於分戒禁立斷遍知</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7260"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若有戒禁亦不同分，於戒禁取亦能隨分立斷遍知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">酉四、不於我語立斷遍知</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">其我語取，於一切時，一切外道悉皆共有，是故外道於自、於他我語取中，皆不施設斷遍知論。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、由未永斷</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又彼雖能分捨諸取，而於當來還復能取，未永斷故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未三、結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是外道，於諸取中未全斷故、未永斷故，不得究竟。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、內法大師</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">內法大師，當知一切與上相違。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是應知內法大師與外道師不同品類。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、沙門義2　寅一、標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7261"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，依四念住修習增上，略由三處、三地、三種補特伽羅，當知普攝諸沙門義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、釋2　卯一、列種類3　辰一、三處</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何三處？一、境，二、智，三、證。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、三地</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何三地？一、正加行攝異生地，二、有學地，三、無學地。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰三、三種補特伽羅</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何三種補特伽羅？一、正加行異生補特伽羅，二、有學補特伽羅，三、無學補特伽羅。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、釋彼相2　辰一、三處攝2　巳一、釋得名3　午一、境</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名境？謂地等六界與六觸處為所依體，此六觸處與十八意行為所依體，十八意行能雜染心。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》與十八意行為所依體者：謂喜、憂、捨，由觸為依各有六種，如是總名十八意行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7262"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、智</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名智？謂心清淨增上慧依處。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》謂心清淨增上慧依處者：此中義顯，心得定故，能如實知、能如實見，乃至能證無作究竟涅槃。由是說慧依處，名心清淨增上。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午三、證</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名證？謂即慧依處增上，若諦依處、若捨依處、若寂依處。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、成依處4　午一、別辨相4　未一、慧依處</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何慧依處？謂慧為依處，於正加行異生地中，正修善法為因緣故，能無放逸入有學地；若慧為依處，證阿羅漢無學地中，得盡智故，如實了知我生盡等；若學無學出世智後諸世間慧。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》云何慧依處等者：此中說慧依處，略有三種。一者、異生正加行慧，二者、有學無漏正慧，三者、有學、無學諸後得慧。如文可知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7263"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、諦依處</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何諦依處？謂已獲得八支聖道，斷諸煩惱。由此依處，當來眾苦畢竟不生。由此畢竟無忘失故，名諦依處。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未三、捨依處</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何捨依處？謂斷彼事。由此依處，於已斷事無雜染行，現法樂住。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》謂斷彼事者：謂於境界事欲已遠離故，彼貪隨眠已永害故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未四、寂依處</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何寂依處？謂為斷滅所餘結事，方便勤修如已得道。此為依處，於所餘結及所餘事，能捨無餘。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、攝要義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是一切以要而言，為欲得證，故修其智；既得證已，便獲聖道及聖道果。果有二種，謂煩惱斷及與事斷。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午三、明施設2　未一、出應作</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7264"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此中一種，證所未證。第二依處，捨未來苦。第三依處，能隨習近現法樂住。第四依處，斷未圓滿能令圓滿。齊爾所處，諸瑜伽師於所應作皆得究竟。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、釋應知</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂於未證，由初能證。於未來苦，第二能捨。於現法樂，第三能住。於上斷滅所未圓滿，第四能滿。如是一切，由四依處應當了知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午四、簡所證</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此中先所獲得聖道，名寂靜道。為斷上位煩惱、事故，正修習時，於其事斷倍趣增益，於煩惱斷防未得退。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》先所獲得聖道名寂靜道者：此中更釋第四名寂依處。由先所獲聖道，名之為寂。以此為依，進斷餘結及餘事故。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此中云何由智觀察所知境界，證所應證？</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼於無明已遠離故，由慧解脫為增上力，依諸明觸所生如理作意相應所有善受，於一切受所生雜染厭捨而住。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">酉二、結應修治</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由於無明觸所生受為緣起貪已遠離故，名得清淨。而於隨眠未永斷故，有餘上位應更修治。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">從此已後，於十八意行無倒觀察，俱於心法同時安住循心法觀。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">天二、思令清淨</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未三、無學位</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼由如是如實知故，漸於見、修所斷三漏，心得解脫。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知此中，一切法者，謂善法欲清淨出家，為證涅槃，先受持戒；由是漸次乃至獲得究竟涅槃。是故宣說一切諸法，欲為根本。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》由是漸次者：謂戒為先，心得靜定；及定為先，證如實智故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、觸</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又依淨戒，引求正法，攝受多聞；由聞正法增上力故，能速集證增語明觸。是故說彼以為觸集。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午三、受</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又彼皆為流趣明觸所生諸受，乃至有餘依般涅槃界為其後際，為求安樂而發起故，此樂一向無罪性故，是故說彼學所攝法為受流趣。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午四、作意</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又彼為求所有明觸，及依明觸所生諸受，起聞思修所成作意，是故說彼為作意生。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7282"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午五、念</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又於爾時，於四念住，由觀品念以觀為依，與內心止為其增上，是故說彼念為增上。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午六、定</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又念增上，起奢摩他，與後聖諦現觀妙智為上首轉，是故說彼定為上首。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午七、慧</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又於聖諦諸現觀中，慧為最勝，謂能無餘永盡諸漏，是故說彼慧為最勝。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午八、解脫</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又由一切漏永盡故，獲得究竟明觸生受俱行解脫。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》獲得究竟明觸生受俱行解脫者：謂阿羅漢慧善解脫故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">即此解脫，非由一切學所攝法數數隨得，唯由頓得。由此解脫，一切樂中為最第一，無罪性故，是故說彼即用解脫以為堅固。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7283"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午九、涅槃</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又彼如是善解脫心，若諸明觸所生受等，若學所攝所有諸法，并所依身，於無餘依般涅槃界，任運自然究竟寂滅，是故說彼皆以涅槃為其後際。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、別配屬2　巳一、配諸學2　午一、別配3　未一、增上戒學</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">應知此中，欲為增上，受持淨戒，名增上戒學。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、增上心學</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">依止觸受增上心慧任持方便所有作意，若念、若定并其加行，名增上心學。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未三、增上慧學</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">慧為最勝，名增上慧學。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、總結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是應知名為三學。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、配學果</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">及彼依持解脫堅固，是有餘依般涅槃界第一學果。涅槃後際，是無餘依般涅槃界第二學果。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7284"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、總結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是略說學及學果，攝一切法。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、諸學學果能證資糧2　卯一、總標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又此諸學及諸學果能證資糧，當知對治八種過患，修習九想。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、別釋2　辰一、釋八過患</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名為八種過患？所謂耽著利養恭敬，愛藏一切後有諸行，懈怠懶惰，薩迦耶見，貪著美味，於諸世間種種妙事欣欲貪愛，依止放逸惡行方便，依止邪願修習梵行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、釋修九想2　巳一、出種類</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名為修習九想。一者、修習出家想，二者、修習無常想，三者、修習無常苦想，四者、修習苦無我想，五者、修習厭逆食想，六者、修習一切世間不可樂想，七者、修習死想，八者、修習世間平等、不平等想，九者、修習有無、出沒、過患、出離想。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7285"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、隨難釋2　午一、釋名平等不平等</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">應知此中，所有如法平等行攝，能往善趣善身語意業，說名平等；所有非法不平等行攝，能往惡趣不善身語意業，名不平等。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、釋名有無出沒等</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又住於此若生若長，能生後際所有眾苦，說名為有。從其前際，於現法中有死滅苦，說名為無。餘出沒等，應知如前已廣分別。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑五、梵行2　寅一、舉外道2　卯一、出不實知2　辰一、於集苦諦2　巳一、別辨相2　午一、初三處</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，諸外道輩，聞不正法增上所生不如理想為依止故，發起無明所生諸受；由此為依，發生諸漏。而諸外道，於是諸漏不如實知，亦於無明觸所生受不如實知，亦於聽聞諸不正法增上所生所有邪想不如實知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、後三處</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7286"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於是三處不實知故，發起欲求，發起有求，亦復發起邪梵行求及無有求。彼於諸欲不如實知，於後有業不如實知，於其眾苦不如實知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、結配屬</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此中前五是集諦處，最後一種是苦諦處。如是外道，於此集諦及以苦諦不如實知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》此中前五是集諦處者：謂如前說，諸漏無明觸所生受，聞不正法所生邪想，及與諸欲，并後有業，皆是集諦攝故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、於染淨相2　巳一、標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又即於此集諦、苦諦，略由二相不如實知。一、雜染故；二、清淨故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、別廣2　午一、雜染</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此中雜染復有四相。一、自性故；二、因故；三、果故；四、因果差別故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、清淨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此中清淨復有二種。一、集苦滅，二、趣滅行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7287"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、斥彼梵行</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼於如是四聖諦中，闕乏正智，不能修習菩提分法，由是因緣，彼所修行所有梵行，不得名為最極究竟；即由此緣，不名究達，不盡漏故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、翻內法</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">住內法者，與彼相違，所修梵行最極究竟，名為究達，盡諸漏故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑六、數取趣2　寅一、總標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="522:73939"></a>復次，於其六種補特伽羅，依染淨法，如來所有大士根智，及當來法生起智轉。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》如來所有大士根智等者：如來無上根勝劣智力，是名大士根智。種種界智力，名當來法生起智。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名六補特伽羅？</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂有一類補特伽羅，先餘生中，於佛善說法毗奈耶獲得淨信，廣說乃至得正直見。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">酉二、今現法行2　戌一、先行放逸</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼於今生，於惡說法毗奈耶中，近不善士，聞不正法，非理作意。於現法中，最初生起諸邪見愛、諸業雜染。彼於爾時，成就前生所有善法，及現法中諸不善法。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復於後時，於善說法毗奈耶中，親近善士，聽聞正法，如理作意。即由先因，棄捨惡說法毗奈耶，於惡說想諸不善法不生染著，速能遣滅。此於當來成清淨法。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">是名第一補特伽羅。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有一類補特伽羅，先餘生中，俱行二法毗奈耶行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》俱行二法毗奈耶行者：謂於善說、惡說二種法毗奈耶中，俱習彼二因行故。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼於今生，最初如前於善說法，乃至獲得如理作意。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》乃至獲得如理作意者：謂如前說，於善說法毗奈耶中，親近善士，聽聞正法，如理作意故。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">是名第二補特伽羅。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未三、第三補特伽羅2　申一、辨2　酉一、先餘生行</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有一類補特伽羅，先餘生中唯行外行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">酉二、今現法行</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼於今生由是為因，串習出家故，串習邪見故，於善說法毗奈耶中，遇緣和合而得出家。既出家已，復生邪見，住自見取，造無間業，亦斷善根，一向成就諸不善法，惡趣決定。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">是名第三補特伽羅。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7291"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、略攝義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是三種補特伽羅，當知第一，先於內法純習因行，於現法中，先行放逸，後不放逸。第二補特伽羅，先於內外俱習因行，於現法中，當知一向行不放逸。第三補特伽羅，先於外法純習因行，於現法中，當知一向多行放逸。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、例後三種2　午一、標相違</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是三種補特伽羅，復有餘三補特伽羅，與上相違，應知其相。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、釋彼相</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此中第一補特伽羅，先於外法純習因行，於現法中，先不放逸，後行放逸。第二補特伽羅，先於內外俱習因行，於現法中，專行放逸。第三補特伽羅，先於內法純習因行，於現法中，當知一向修不放逸。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、釋染淨法相4　辰一、因緣差別喻2　巳一、約善說辨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又於此中，先世所習善不善因，猶如種子。今世善說法毗奈耶，於其先世諸善種子，猶如良田；於彼先世不善種子，猶如瘠田。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7292"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、約惡說辨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">與是相違，今世惡說法毗奈耶，於其先世不善種子，猶如良田；於彼先世諸善種子，猶如瘠田。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》又於此中先世所習善不善因等者：此中舉喻，種種差別。義顯前說先於內外俱習因行二種補特伽羅，諸所有相應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、能治所治喻</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又彼先世因增上力，今善法起，猶如光明，與彼一切如無明闇諸不善法為能對治；彼不善法，與彼一切猶如光明所有善法為所對治。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰三、能燒所燒喻2　巳一、約不善法辨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是先世諸不善法，如有熱炭，由有能燒身心義故。今世惡說法毗奈耶，如乾葦舍。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、約善法辨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又彼先世所有善法，如有熱炭，由有能燒煩惱義故。今世善說法毗奈耶，如乾葦舍。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7293"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰四、依處差別喻2　巳一、約善法辨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又彼先世所有善法，處今惡說法毗奈耶，由損減故，猶如置在冷地石器，如無熱炭。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、約不善法辨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又彼先世諸不善法，處今善說法毗奈耶，由斷滅故，猶如置在冷地石器，如無熱炭。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、顯如來希有</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="522:73996"></a>此中諸如來，由大士無上根勝劣智力，於其先世善不善因所習成根，隨其所應如實了知；又於現法染淨門轉，生起當來染淨諸法，亦隨所應如實了知；故言成就甚奇希有。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑七、超3　寅一、總標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7294"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，往惡趣行、往善趣行超度差別，當知略有五門不同。由此五門，於自超度如實了知，於他超度亦正遍知，所謂諸佛及佛弟子。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、別釋5　卯一、永損害門2　辰一、出往惡趣行3　巳一、徵</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名為往惡趣行？</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂諸外道，所有一切薩迦耶見以為根本諸惡見趣，并彼所緣、并彼所依以為依止，發生種種惡欲及害，若殺生等所有無量惡不善法，如經廣說，乃至所有諸非法行、不平等行以為最後。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》乃至所有諸非法行等者：謂諸外道，依邪行處引隨煩惱。略有三種，謂隨逐遠離、隨逐憒鬧、隨逐學處起隨煩惱。如是一切，總略說名非法之行、不平等行。由非善義，名非法行；非愛果義，名不平等行。義如三摩呬多地決擇中說。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/62.htm#74004:49560">陵本六十二卷三頁4974</a>）</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">能往險惡處，能往那落迦，能往諸惡趣，差別生起。若往於彼，名生惡趣，領受彼因所感非愛諸果異熟。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7295"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是名為往惡趣行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、明能永害道2　巳一、由現觀3　午一、出觀相</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於此多聞諸聖弟子，若彼所緣生諸見趣，若自所依令起執著，</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若諸所有能往一切險惡趣等諸惡欲等，廣說乃至諸非法行、不平等行以為最後，若往於彼領受非愛險惡等果，如是一切，如實隨觀非我、我所。謂於是中，決定無我，亦無我所。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是觀已，當於聖諦得現觀時，彼諸見趣隨眠根本皆永拔故，說名為斷，其餘一切畢竟不續。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午三、結永害</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7296"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此聖弟子，於彼見趣以為根本所有能往險惡處等，定不能作，定不能往險惡處等。是名第一往惡趣行永損害門。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、由遍知2　午一、辨2　未一、異生</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由是因緣，能於自內如實了知：離我等聖所餘異生，雖復有能以世間道，超度能往惡趣不善及惡趣等，獲得四種現法樂住，或得超過諸色無色寂靜解脫，然其不能究竟損害，諸惡趣等後可相應。是故彼流，雖極能離欲、色界愛，暫時獲得勝上樂住，而復當來更還造作殺生等事，往諸惡趣。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">我等定當不能造作殺生等事，乃至廣說諸非法行、不平等行，我等定當能不造作。是名聖法毗奈耶中永損害門，謂能損害往惡趣行。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是諸佛及佛弟子，能實遍知永損害門所有差別。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、發心願門</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7297"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又即如是諸聖弟子，為欲超度所餘未斷往善趣行，此聖弟子，於先所作不生喜足，於上漏盡起欣樂欲，發正願心。於彼所得諸世俗道，審觀過患，謂彼不能究竟離苦。是名第一為欲超度往善趣行發心願門。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">發心願已，普於一切善趣後有所生愛味深觀過患，如險惡道，心生厭離；欣慕寂靜現法涅槃，正修方便。由是進趣如先所得趣涅槃行。如是名為能進趣門。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼由修道漸次離欲，乃至能入第一有定。若於上捨多生愛味放逸因緣，於現法中不般涅槃，但名上行不還果者。如是名為後上行門。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、明得解脫2　午一、修方便</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若於念住善安住心，如實了知此所生起隨煩惱已，</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》此所生起隨煩惱者：此隨煩惱，謂即惛沈下劣應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">便從內聚還收其心，安置在外淨妙境相，謂於佛等功德行緣持心令住。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》謂於佛等功德行緣等者：謂於佛法僧勝功德田作意思惟，發生歡喜故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由緣此故，發生歡喜；廣說乃至由妙舉門，於所緣境令心得定，從奢摩他之所對治諸隨煩惱而得解脫。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7320"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》由妙舉門於所緣境令心得定者：謂由淨妙所緣境界，策勵其心，及彼隨順發勤精進，是故能令心得正定。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、如實知</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">從此已後，如實了知於隨煩惱心得解脫。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、後位2　巳一、於外解脫</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">為此義故，祈願於外；</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》為此義故等者：為欲解脫隨煩惱故，名為此義。於外淨妙境相安止其心，是名祈願於外。由是於隨煩惱心得解脫，名得此義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">得此義已，還復如前攝心內聚，而不為其諸隨煩惱之所惱亂。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》諸隨煩惱之所惱亂者：貪欲等五，是名諸隨煩惱。故能令心恆不寂靜，名所惱亂。今翻彼義應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">心內聚已，不由祈願，自然如實了知於外心得解脫。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、成辦勝定</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7321"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼於外緣行相、尋思，有所制伏，有其加行，難可運轉，皆得自在解脫棄捨安樂而住，已得成辦勝奢摩他。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》彼於外緣行相尋思等者：此中行相，謂色等十。言尋思者，謂欲等八。如是二種，緣外門轉，故名外緣。於彼一切取過患想，名有制伏及有加行，由是說彼難可運轉。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、結差別</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是彼於四種念住善安住心，能正了知前後差別。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、出毗鉢舍那</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又應知此補特伽羅，先已修行毗鉢舍那，毗鉢舍那以為依止，於奢摩他修瑜伽行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑四、取相3　寅一、出所應</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7322"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，有諸苾芻，於諸念住勤修加行，毗鉢舍那以為依止，於奢摩他樂修觀行。彼即應於內奢摩他所攝自心，取如是相：謂我今者何所思惟，云何思惟，令奢摩他所攝受心，為奢摩他所治身心惛沈下劣之所惱亂。復我今者何所思惟，云何思惟，令奢摩他所攝受心，不為彼法之所惱亂。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、辨失德2　卯一、舉有失2　辰一、隨惑所惱不得靜定2　巳一、標義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若彼苾芻不取如是自心相貌，但自了知此隨煩惱染汙心已，便於外緣取淨妙相。由是為因，雖能暫時除遣現在現前隨惑，然於後時，若復如前攝心內聚，還為如是隨惑所惱，不得靜定。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、釋相</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如先不取自心相故，由是因緣，為隨煩惱數數擾亂；又不能得所欣求義，復為憂愁之所損惱。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、不獲寂止及正念知</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又經長時，不能獲得內心寂止，不能獲得依奢摩他、毗鉢舍那為先清淨增上第一正念正知。由不獲得內心寂止，故不能得四增上心現法樂住；</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7323"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》四增上心現法樂住者：四靜慮定，名四增上心。依諸靜慮，於現法中領受喜樂、安樂、捨樂，是名現法樂住。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由不獲得增上第一正念正知，故不能得先所未得無上安隱究竟涅槃。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、翻有德</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">與上相違，應知即是一切白品，乃至獲得先所未得無上安隱究竟涅槃。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、舉譬喻</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此中典廚譬瑜伽師，主即譬於內奢摩他所攝受心，其餚膳味喻執取相，上妙衣食喻於內心奢摩他等，當知黑品喻諸愚夫，所有白品喻諸智者。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑五、諸纏2　寅一、異生2　卯一、出貪纏</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，有諸苾芻，於諸念住正勤修習，而是異生。或有勝妙可愛境界正現在前，或復獨處得諸相狀，由失念故，不如理想以為依止，率爾發起猛利貪纏。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、明對治2　辰一、由深厭恥</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7324"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼於此纏深心厭恥，謂如自身墮於厄難極鄙穢處，發起猛利思遠離心。由如是行，便於彼纏心得解脫；既解脫已，心生歡喜。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、由無常想</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">從此已後，起猛利厭；猛利厭後，得無常想，如見大犁發諸行塊，便於聖諦如實現觀，以其依止依附涅槃。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、有學3　卯一、出貪纏</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又即有學觀察作意，於勝妙境思惟淨相，由未永斷貪隨眠故，貪纏率爾生起現前。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、明對治</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">尋復於彼深見過患，為欲斷此纏及隨眠，入無相定，如是能斷餘未斷法。從定起已，如實了知一切已斷，領受微妙解脫喜樂。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》領受微妙解脫喜樂者：無漏界中，離貪、離瞋、離癡等欲，證勝義樂，是名微妙解脫喜樂。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、辨勝劣</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="97p7325"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如實觀見自己成就大智力故，名為彊盛。諸魔羅品其力羸劣。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑六、果利</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，修四念住所引功德，當知能感最勝增上究竟果故，名有大果；當知能感最勝增上樂勝利故，名有大利。</span></strong></span></span><br />
