<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　彌勒菩薩說</span></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7185"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　唐三藏沙門玄奘奉詔譯</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　韓清淨科記</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">攝事分中契經事緣起食諦界擇攝第三之四</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">壬四、界攝2　癸一、總嗢柁南標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，總嗢柁南曰：</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　總義等光等　受等最為後</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">癸二、別嗢柁南辨3　子一、總義等2　丑一、頌標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">別嗢柁南曰：</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　總義自類別　似轉後三求</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、長行釋4　寅一、總義2　卯一、標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="44564:72906"></a>當知諸界略有二種。一、住自性界，二、習增長界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7186"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、隨釋2　辰一、住自性界</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="53293:72909"></a>住自性界者，謂十八界，墮自相續，各各決定差別種子。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、習增長界</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">習增長界者，謂則諸法或是其善，或是不善，於餘生中先已數習，令彼現行，故於今時種子彊盛，依附相續。由是為因，暫遇小緣便能現起，定不可轉。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》定不可轉者：謂無堪能轉變彼種令不現起故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、自類別3　卯一、總標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，以要言之，雖界種類十八可得，然一一界業趣、有情，種種品類有差別故，當知無量。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、舉喻</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">譬如世間大惡叉聚，於此聚中有多品類，種類一故，雖說為一，而有無量。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、合法</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7187"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是於其一一界中，各有無量品類差別，種類一故，雖各說一，而實無量。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、似轉2　卯一、標差別</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，如是諸界，由勝解力之所集成。先惡勝解，集成惡界；先善勝解，集成善界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、明似轉2　辰一、標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">隨所集成，還與如是相似有情同法而轉。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、釋</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂相往來、同聚、同住、同見、同意勝解相似。由是故言：有情諸界共相滋潤、相似而轉。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅四、三求3　卯一、依梵行求3　辰一、出學漸次2　巳一、別辨相</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，由梵行求增上力故，先說起信；次於尸羅受學而轉；次於現行所有過罪，觀自、觀他而生羞恥；次於善法無間修習，發勤精進，於久所作及久所說，能無忘失；是二為依，令心得定；由心定故，得如實智。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7188"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、略攝義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是且說信增上力，漸次修習三種所學。一、增上戒，二、增上心，三、增上慧。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、名得圓滿</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是三學勝資糧道，謂世正見、好行惠捨、易養、易滿、少欲、喜足及四攝事。其易養等句義差別，如聲聞地已說其相。如是當知名梵行求已得圓滿。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》謂世正見等者：謂依增上聖正言教，發生如理所引作意，名世正見。為莊嚴心、為伴助心、為資瑜伽、為得上義而修布施，是名惠捨。易養、易滿、少欲、喜足，是名成就沙門莊嚴。聲聞地中別釋其相應知。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/25.htm#72933:21112">陵本二十五卷十八頁2143</a>）</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰三、明安立界2　巳一、顯差別</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">成就如是梵行求者，還與此界諸有情類共相滋潤、相似而轉。離此界者，還與遠離此界有情共相滋潤、相似而轉。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7189"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、隨難釋</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知此中，果依於因，非因依果故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》當知此中果依於因等者：諸有情類共相滋潤、相似而轉，是名為果。若梵行求成就，或不成就，是名為因。果依因故，名相似轉，當知非謂因依於果。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、依有求2　辰一、辨為緣</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">無明界所隨六處諸界為緣所依別故，起無明觸種種品類；其無明觸種種品類以為緣故，起無明觸所生諸受種種品類；其無明觸所生諸受種種品類以為緣故，起無明觸所生諸受為緣貪愛；愛為緣故，而有其取；廣說乃至大苦蘊集。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、結建立</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知是名依有求故，建立諸界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7190"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》無明界所隨六處諸界等者：六處種子為無明種子之所隨逐，是故六處為無明所緣，及為無明之所依處。由是生起眼乃至意諸無明觸，名無明觸種種品類。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、依欲求2　辰一、辨為緣</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又無明界所隨六處諸界為緣，起無明觸；此無明觸以為緣故，於諸境界起不如理執取相、好所有諸想；此想為緣，於諸境界發起希欲；希欲為緣，起彼隨法多隨尋思；由彼隨法多隨尋思以為緣故，發起思慕愁憂所作身心熱惱；身心熱惱以為緣故，於諸境界種種品類思求差別皆可了知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、結建立</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是當知依欲求故，安立諸界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子二、光等2　丑一、頌標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，嗢柁南曰：</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　三七界相攝　見想與希奇　差別性安立　寂靜愚夫後</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、長行釋8　寅一、三七界相攝3　卯一、出界差別2　辰一、三界</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7191"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">界有三種。一者、色界，二、無色界，三者、滅界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、七界</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有七界。一、光明界，二、清淨界，三、空處界，四、識處界，五、無所有處界，六、非想非非想處界，七、滅界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、明其相攝2　辰一、具攝一切</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知此中，由其色界攝光明界及清淨界，由無色界攝四無色，由其滅界還攝滅界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、隨釋初二</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又諸色貪，由見、由受所顯發故，遍於一切色界地中，安立光明及清淨界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》又諸色貪由見由受所顯發故者：謂於諸色推求、領受，故起色貪，是名由見、由受之所顯發。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、廣遍知等2　辰一、標應知</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又於如是七界遍知應當了知，於得方便應當了知，即於其得應當了知，於得所為應當了知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7192"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、別釋相4　巳一、於界遍知2　午一、標列因緣</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是諸界所有遍知，由四因緣應當了知。謂有相違所治、能治而相待故，狹小、無量而相待故，有及非有而相待故，有上、無上而相待故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、別釋其相4　未一、待所治能治</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">黑闇為緣，施設光明；不淨為緣，施設清淨；色趣為緣，施設虛空。如是名為有相違故，待彼所治，施設能治。由待彼故，能於此中正覺慧轉。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、待狹小無量</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由緣有量狹小境識以為緣故，施設識無邊處。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未三、待有非有</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由少所有以為緣故，施設無所有處。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未四、待有上無上2　申一、出二種</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7193"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由一切有最勝現前以為緣故，施設非想非非想處為有無上；由薩迦耶所有相應諸煩惱斷以為緣故，施設滅界為滅無上。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、簡差別</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知有頂是有無上，滅於諸法皆是無上。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、於得方便2　午一、由有行定2　未一、標得名</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又有想定名為有行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、明獲得2　申一、標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於七界中，次第乃至無所有處，一切皆是有想定故，皆由行定隨順獲得。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、釋2　酉一、舉光明想</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂取明相光明想俱，修三摩地，隨順獲得光明想定。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">酉二、例清淨等</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是由取清淨、虛空、識無邊想、無所有想，當知亦爾。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、不由行定2　未一、別辨2　申一、獲得第一有定</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7194"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">非想非非想處，由無相作意方便趣入，想極細故，取為第一。諸有寂靜起勝解時，隨順獲得第一有定。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、獲得滅定滅界</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於一切相不思惟故，於無相界正思惟故，薩迦耶滅。由無相故，隨順獲得滅定、滅界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、總結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是二種，不由行定隨順獲得。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、於得諸界</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、隨難釋</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">一切皆為趣向涅槃，悉以涅槃為其後際，熟修梵行，是故過此無復立界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、餘善巧2　巳一、指其三種</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、辨處非處3　午一、標依二行</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、略辨三種</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午三、廣釋所以2　未一、趣善趣行2　申一、標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">所以者何？趣善趣行此最為極，更無餘行，唯此能感所有世間最極圓滿。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、釋</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼復云何？</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">戌三、釋</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂於是處，正見具足補特伽羅不能現行，諸異生類堪任現行。當知一切如經廣說。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》謂於是處正見具足補特伽羅不能現行者：此中正見，謂加行位，世間所攝。能成就此，補特伽羅雖歷千生，不墮惡趣。以是義說，能往彼趣諸業、煩惱不能現行。此即釋前安住菩提劣功德者，於諸世間為殊勝義。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》三品類觸是受因緣者：苦觸、樂觸、不苦不樂觸，名三品類觸應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、見2　辰一、出應觀3　巳一、於樂受</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又諸樂受變壞法故，貪依處故，貪是當來眾苦因故，由此應觀樂受為苦。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、於苦受</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若諸苦受現在前時，惱害性故，如中毒箭而未得拔，由此應觀苦受如箭。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、於不苦不樂受</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7226"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">非苦樂受，已滅壞者是無常故，正現前者是滅法故，於二更續能隨順故，由此應觀非苦樂受性是無常、性是滅法。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、結悟入</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是於受所生正見，能隨悟入諸有所受皆悉是苦。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯四、染2　辰一、標名雜染</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於樂受中有貪隨眠，於苦受中有瞋隨眠，於非苦樂無明隨眠，是名於受所起雜染。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">雖於樂等所有諸受現前分位，一切未斷煩惱隨眠之所隨眠，然由緣彼各別所行諸纏生起，此後隨眠煩惱隨縛，即名於彼相續隨眠。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》然由緣彼各別所行等者：謂若可意、不可意等差別，是名各別所行。緣彼三種，如次生起貪瞋癡纏。</span></strong></span></span><br />

<a name="96p7231"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又麤尋伺能發語言，諸未得定、或有已得還從定起，能發語言，非正在定。正在定者，雖有微細尋伺隨轉，而不能發所有語言，是故此位說名一切語言寂靜。是名第二義門差別。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、釋止息</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又瑜伽師，於貪瞋癡深見過患，安住領納貪瞋癡等離繫諸受，數數遍知、數數斷滅貪瞋癡等，故說其心於貪瞋癡離染解脫。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯十、觀察</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又由七行，於諸受中觀受七相。謂觀諸受自性故，現在流轉、還滅因緣故，當來流轉因緣故，當來還滅因緣故，雜染因緣故，清淨因緣故，及清淨故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7232"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》又由七行於諸受中觀受七相等者：決擇分說：一切如來應正等覺出現世間，皆於諸受起八種觀。謂受有幾種？誰為受集？誰是受滅？誰是受集趣行？誰是受滅趣行？誰是受愛味？誰是受過患？誰是受出離？乃至廣說。如是八種觀察諸受，當知略顯自相觀、現法轉因觀、彼滅觀、後法轉因觀、彼滅觀、彼二轉因觀、彼二轉滅因觀，及清淨觀。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/53.htm#73394:41598">陵本五十三卷十八頁4271</a>）今說七行與彼相攝，如應當知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、第二嗢柁南攝2　寅一、頌標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，嗢柁南曰：</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　受生起劣等　諸受相差別　見等為最勝　知差別問記</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、長行釋7　卯一、受生起3　辰一、略標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">一切有情應斷諸受，略由三緣而得生起。一者、欲緣，謂於未來世。二者、尋緣，謂於過去世。三者、觸緣，謂於現在世現前境界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、別釋相2　巳一、出有情眾4　午一、徵</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名為一切有情？</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、標</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">謂有情眾略有八種。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午三、列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7233"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">一、在家眾。二、出家眾。三、於諸欲未離貪眾。四、於諸欲已離貪眾。五、於初靜慮未離貪眾。六、於初靜慮已離貪眾。七、從此已上，乃至非想非非想處未得離貪諸外道眾，能入世間定，具足於邪見乃至邪解脫智者。八、住內法眾，能入世間定，具足於正見乃至正解脫智者；及住內法眾，能入出世定者。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午四、結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由此八眾，依能領納諸受遍知，應知普攝諸有情眾。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》謂有情眾略有八種等者：此中諸欲未離貪、已離貪眾，義顯欲為受緣。於初靜慮未離貪、已離貪眾，義顯尋為受緣。具足邪見、正見等眾，義顯觸為受緣。在家、出家通攝一切，故作如是八種分別。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、配諸受緣4　午一、未離貪已離貪眾2　未一、總標三緣</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又在家眾、或出家眾，於諸欲中未離貪者，由三因緣，諸染汙受而得生起。一、由染著力，二、由作意力，三、由境界力。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7234"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》又在家眾或出家眾等者：此中三種因緣，如次配釋欲、尋、觸三。然於此中，唯說於諸欲中未離貪者，當知舉初兼攝後二。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、別釋二眾2　申一、在家眾2　酉一、第一義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知此中，諸在家者追求諸欲，為受用故發生欲樂，由染著力。即此非理思惟先時曾所領受，由作意力。於現前境現在受用，由境界力。應知如是補特伽羅欲、尋、觸緣，由現行故，皆不寂靜，以此為緣，發生三受。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">酉二、第二義2　戌一、釋2　亥一、明隨轉</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又由最初染汙欲、尋、觸現行故，領納彼緣所生諸受。若彼生已，染著不捨，亦不除遣。如是彼受長時相續隨轉不絕，不得寂靜；不寂靜緣，長時相續領納諸受。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》又由最初染汙欲尋觸現行等者：前說由三因緣，染受生起，即由此義名為最初。從是以後，長時隨轉，是名相續。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">亥二、名為緣</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7235"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又彼欲等，由其最初長時相續恆現行故，彼緣彼品所有煩惱墮在相續未永斷故，即說名為不寂靜緣。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">戌二、結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">是名第二義門差別。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、出家眾2　酉一、第一義2　戌一、辨2　亥一、未離貪2　天一、尋觸現行</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若諸出家未離貪者，由於諸欲能棄捨故，其染著力所攝受欲雖得寂靜，作意、境界力所攝受，若尋、若觸而未寂靜。由是因緣，彼於獨處，於尋對治未善修故，一切離欲皆未作故，於曾受境，非理作意尋思現行；於諸勝妙現前境界，有觸現行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">天二、唯觸現行</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若於尋思深見過失，於彼對治已善修故，一切離欲未盡作故，欲如前說已得寂靜。由是因緣，尋亦寂靜，唯觸獨一未得寂靜。若勝妙境現在前時，諸染汙觸便復生起。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7236"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">亥二、已離貪</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若於諸欲已離貪者，當知一切皆得寂靜。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">戌二、結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">是名一種義門差別。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">酉二、第二義2　戌一、辨4　亥一、貪欲尋觸皆未寂靜</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有一類，於諸欲中未離貪者，由於諸欲所有貪欲未永斷故，諸尋、染觸未永斷故，由是一切皆未寂靜。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">亥二、欲已寂靜尋未寂靜</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若於諸欲貪欲已斷，證初靜慮，欲已寂靜，尋未寂靜。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">亥三、欲尋寂靜觸未寂靜</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於初靜慮已離貪者，乃至非想非非想處未離貪者，二已寂靜，觸未寂靜。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">亥四、貪欲尋觸一切寂靜</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">超過有頂，一切寂靜。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7237"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">戌二、結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">是名第二義門差別。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、入世間定諸外道眾</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若諸外道，能入世間定，具足於邪見乃至邪解脫智者，由彼為緣生起諸受，於彼染著。又由彼品煩惱隨縛，即由如是不寂靜緣，諸受生起。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午三、入世間定諸內法眾</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若住內法，能入世間定，具足於正見乃至正解脫智者，由彼為緣生起諸受，於彼染著。又由彼品煩惱隨縛，即由如是不寂靜緣，諸受生起。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午四、入出世定諸內法眾2　未一、明彼欲2　申一、未寂靜</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又住內法，能入出世定者，若依向道轉，自事未究竟，所有諸欲未得為得、未證為證、未觸為觸。作是希望：我於是處何時當得？廣說如前。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》廣說如前者：如前未證為證，未觸為觸。此應廣說何時當證、當觸故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼未寂靜，由是為緣，彼於爾時諸受生起。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7238"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、已寂靜</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若於自事已得究竟，彼欲寂靜。由寂靜緣，便有第一寂靜無上諸受生起。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、出勝義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼於一切所有諸受出離方便如實了知，是故如前於第一義諸沙門中，許為沙門；諸梵志中，許為梵志。若不了知，於彼一切皆不忍許。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰三、總料簡4　巳一、苦法攝2　午一、無差別緣</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知此中，一切諸受無有差別，皆觸為緣。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、有差別緣</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又即此緣欲亦為緣，尋亦為緣，境界愚癡所攝無明亦為其緣。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》又即此緣欲亦為緣等者：此中義顯，諸受有差別緣應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、集法攝</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是一切不正思惟，及墮相續彼品煩惱，以為其集。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、滅法攝</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7239"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由此滅故，彼亦隨滅。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳四、道法攝</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">正見等道，當知說名能趣滅行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、劣等3　辰一、於初靜慮</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，於遠離喜身作證住諸聖弟子，能斷五法，能修五法令得圓滿。應知如前三摩呬多地廣辨其相。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》能斷五法等者：三摩呬多地說：初靜慮中說離生喜，由證住此，斷除五法。謂欲所引喜、欲所引憂、不善所引喜、不善所引憂、不善所引捨。又於五法修習圓滿。謂歡、喜、安、樂及三摩地。乃至廣說。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/11.htm#73471:8227">陵本十一卷二頁838</a>）其義應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、於欲界及四靜慮</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又喜樂捨劣中勝品，謂在欲界及四靜慮。如其所應，當知其相。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰三、於第四靜慮及在上地</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7240"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又在第四靜慮地捨，一切過患皆遠離故，名善清淨。若此上捨，復可立為勝無愛味。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、諸受相差別2　辰一、標列十相</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯七、記2　辰一、出二記別2　巳一、標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，諸佛如來有二記別。一、共外道，二者、不共。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》諸佛如來有二記別者：謂依二諦，有二記別。依世俗諦，說共外道；依勝義諦，說與不共。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、隨釋2　午一、共外道</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">共外道者，記諸弟子當生處等。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、不共外道2　未一、簡他</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">言不共者，終不記別有生者等。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、顯自2　申一、明識依住2　酉一、標列二依</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7250"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">有二識火熾然所依。一、微細愛，二、麤名色。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">酉二、釋依住相3　戌一、欲色界識</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">欲、色二界愛所生識，名色為依。愛若止息，乃至壽量，其識相續隨轉而住。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">戌二、無色界識</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若無色界愛所生識，但緣其名而得住立。愛若斷滅，乃至壽量，其識相續隨轉而住。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">戌三、色中有識</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又於色界此愛為依，生中有識。即愛為依，令於中有般涅槃者，暫爾安住。此愛若斷，即於爾時其識謝滅。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、辨意生身</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有二種意所生身。一者、色界意所生身，二、無色界意所生身。謂由定地意門方便，而能集成二生身故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、明善避他2　巳一、標列二論</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="96p7251"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又諸如來，略有二種善避他論。一者、能避定不應記作不定論，二者、能避決定應記作不定論。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、隨釋後一</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如說喜樂色等義別，如是喜樂取等義別，應知亦爾。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》如說喜樂色等義別等者：此中義顯決定記事。謂唯有心住四識住，有轉有染；又唯有心斷四識住，無轉無染。心轉染時，復有四依，謂色、受、想、行，由是此說色等義別。復有四取，謂於欲、見、戒禁、我語所有欲貪，由是此說取等義別。言喜樂者，謂喜為先因，於彼彼生處障於厭離，滋潤自體。如是一切，已如前說應知。（<a href="http://mahabodhi.org/yoga/cb/87.htm#73598:66539">陵本八十七卷十三頁6588</a>）</span></strong></span></span><br />
