<h3 style="text-align:start"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkred"><strong>瑜伽師地論科句披尋記卷第九十四</strong></span></span></span></span></h3>

<p><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　彌勒菩薩說</span></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7059"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　唐三藏沙門玄奘奉詔譯</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　韓清淨科記</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">攝事分中契經事緣起食諦界擇攝第三之二</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子三、觸為緣等2　丑一、頌標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，嗢柁南曰：</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">　觸緣見圓滿　實解不愛樂　法住智精進　生處等為後</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、長行釋8　寅一、觸緣2　卯一、標列三記</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於一切觸緣受有中，若諸沙門、或婆羅門，宣說無因、惡因論者，如前請問，此作此受，乃至廣說。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》乃至廣說者：謂餘作餘受應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7060"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">安住正法大師弟子，若勝、若劣，略有三種無倒記別。一、開自宗記，二、伏他宗記，三、有執無執雜染清淨記。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、隨釋差別3　辰一、開自宗記</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知此中，於彼所問無差別記：謂諸苦樂皆從緣生，是我宗致。斯則名為開自宗記。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、伏他宗記2　巳一、出能伏2　午一、舉記</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若於彼問作如是記：諸計苦樂自作、他作、俱作、俱非無因而生，於一切處由觸生受，何用妄計自他作等。若觸因受現不可得，更求餘因可為巧妙；然觸因受既現可得，故求餘因非為巧妙。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、結名</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是記者，是則名為伏他宗記。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、釋所以2　午一、第一義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">所以者何？由二因緣，彼為摧伏。一者、除唯根、境、識合，不能顯示餘作者故；二者、不能誹撥一切世間現量如理所得觸因緣故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7061"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、第二義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又彼不能立自宗故，亦復不能破他宗故，名被摧伏。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰三、有執無執離染清淨記4　巳一、舉記</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若於彼問作如是記：我亦唯依根、境界、識，假立自作、他作、俱作若苦若樂，而於實我都無所執；汝於此中有邪執著，故不隨許。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、釋因</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">所以者何？若有執著即為雜染，若無執著即為清淨。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、辨相2　午一、雜染</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名為若有執著即為雜染？謂彼世間不聰慧者，若於前際有所執著，無明緣行，廣說如前，便於中際苦樂雜染；若於中際有所執著，彼亦如前，當於後際苦樂雜染。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7062"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》若於前際有所執著等者：謂於前際諸行執我、我所，由是無明為緣生行，廣說如前至觸緣受，便於中際苦樂雜染。如是若於中際有所執著，無明緣行，廣說如前至觸緣受，當於後際苦樂雜染。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、清淨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名為若無執著即為清淨？謂聰慧者，若於前際、或於中際，不於諸行執我、我所，彼於前際諸受因滅，已般涅槃；或於後際諸受因滅，當般涅槃。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳四、結名</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">是名第三有執無執雜染清淨記。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、見圓滿3　卯一、出見差別2　辰一、釋行相3　巳一、名見圓滿</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，若有棄捨無因、惡因，於因生法五種因中獲得正見，名見圓滿。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、名正直見</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於此正法及毗奈耶不可轉故，亦得名為成正直見。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、名成證淨</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由於涅槃意樂淨故，亦名成就於佛證淨，於所知境智清淨故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、配所緣</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7063"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由此三緣，如其次第，名於正法趣向、親近及與正證。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》由此三緣等者：謂由於因生法五種因中獲得正見，名於正法趣向。於此正法及毗奈耶不可轉故，名於正法親近。涅槃意樂淨故，名於正法正證。如是次第應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、釋五種因2　辰一、徵起</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">云何名為從因生法五種因耶？</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、列釋5　巳一、惡趣因</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">一、惡趣因，謂諸不善及不善根。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、善趣因</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">二、善趣因，謂一切善及諸善根。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、識住因</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">三、於識住令識住因，謂四種食。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳四、雜染因</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7064"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">四、現法後法雜染因，謂一切漏。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳五、清淨因</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">五、清淨因，謂諦緣起。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、釋見圓滿2　辰一、標名</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若有於此諸因自性，如實了知是其自性；於此因緣，如實了知是其因緣；於因緣滅，如實了知真實是滅；於趣滅道，如實了知真實是道；名見圓滿。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、顯義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">觀緣生事，乃至無明為邊際故，過此更無緣生因觀，唯由此觀，自義究竟。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、實2　卯一、標列境事</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，略有三種於現法中真實寂滅，乃至壽量未永止息，恆相續轉所知境事。於彼有學正修行時，施設學性。於彼無學作是思惟：我一切盡，不復當盡。盡、無生智所思擇故，名思擇法。云何為三？一、六處，二、六處緣觸，三、觸緣受。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7065"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、釋其施設2　辰一、由補特伽羅增上2　巳一、第一義2　午一、學常委2　未一、釋修相2　申一、別辨三種2　酉一、於受</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知此中，所有多聞諸聖弟子，隨所領受，即於彼受如實遍知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》所有多聞諸聖弟子者：此中多聞諸聖弟子，謂諸異生。若唯說聖弟子，謂已見諦。如是二種皆有學攝，如下自釋應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又即於彼厭離欲滅勤修正行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">酉二、於觸六處</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又能如實了知彼受觸所引生，觸復由彼六處引生，即於彼觸引因六處厭離欲滅勤修正行。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">申二、略攝無常</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又於彼受、觸及六處一切實事略攝為一，了知一切由無常滅名滅法已，於現法中，於此一切三種實事無常滅法，如前修行厭離欲滅。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、結得名</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7066"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由此正行，名學常委。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、擇法常委</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又由修行此正行故，無所造作，究竟解脫，是故說名擇法常委。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、第二義2　午一、學常委</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">為欲證得曾所未得、曾所未證，修行無間、殷重方便，名學常委。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、擇法常委</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">為於所有現法樂住無有退失無間所作、殷重所作，由是說名擇法常委。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、由法增上2　巳一、名法界</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">等說一切事法增上名句文身，名為法界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》等說一切事法增上名句文身者：無倒名句文身，能令解了一切事法，是名增上，如其法性平等說故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、名善達法界2　午一、由無礙解</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">諸有獲得無礙解故，名句文身隨欲自在，是故說名善達法界。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7067"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、能廣宣說2　未一、於真實義</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、根圓滿2　未一、舉種類</span></strong></span></span><br />

<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、釋</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">然唯約與未生諸法作生緣理，唯法引法說為食義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、名食者3　寅一、標唯假</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7113"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">但由法假，於其識上假想施設補特伽羅，望此四食說為食者，為欲隨順世間言說。約世俗諦，說有如是補特伽羅，能食四食，非約勝義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、釋所以2　卯一、明非理</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">所以者何？若說有識生已安住，體是真實補特伽羅，名能食者，不應立識為其食性。未曾見有補特伽羅，還自能食補特伽羅。一相續中，定無二識同時安住。是故立識體是真實補特伽羅，為能食者，不應道理。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、記二問2　辰一、斥他</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由有如是不應理故，若作是問：誰食識食？當知此問為非理問。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、顯自</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若作是問：誰是能食識食因緣？當知此問為如理問，能令悟入緣起理故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》誰是能食識食因緣等者：此中義顯，諸業、煩惱，名為能食識食因緣。如下釋義應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅三、明緣起2　卯一、辨流轉2　辰一、標列有二</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7114"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復有二有。一者、生有，二者、業有。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、釋其相續2　巳一、由業有趣生有2　午一、出名業有</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若為當來後有生起，今現法中，諸業、煩惱所隨逐識為因，能引當來生有。即彼曾有前行業性，說名業有。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、顯趣生有</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於現法中有此有故，能令當來生有所攝後有生起。於命終時，前際六處纔無常滅，後際六處尋復續生。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》於現法中有此有故者：謂有業有應知。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、由生有成業有</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">即此六處，識於先時為能引緣，復於今時為結生緣。如是由識入母胎故，得有名色；名色為緣，便有六處；由無明界所隨六處以為緣故，有相似觸；漸次乃至取為緣故，令後際業轉成其有。如是諸法先未曾有，一切新從別別緣起。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7115"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、結略義2　辰一、簡無補特伽羅</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知此中，都無觸者乃至有者能有所觸，乃至有有。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、顯唯諸法假立3　巳一、標因果</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">唯有諸法別名所食、別名能食，是故因果墮在諸行相續流轉，無有斷絕。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、釋相續</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由其先際業有，往趣後際生有；復由後際業有，還趣先際生有。如是緣起輪迴不絕，從此世間往彼世間，自彼世間還此世間。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》由其先際業有等者：此中義顯業有為依，往趣生有，此彼世間輪轉不絕，故作是說。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳三、結食義</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">是故唯法能引法義，當知此中說為食義。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子四、極多諸過患3　丑一、標列苦因</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7116"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><a name="52573:72199"></a>復次，三食為因，能令三種內苦生起。一者、界不平等所生病苦，二者、欲希求苦，三者、求不允苦。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、正觀過患2　寅一、於前三食2　卯一、辨3　辰一、於段食</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">初苦，段食為因；第二苦，觸食為因；第三苦，意會思食為因。段食因緣，生內病苦，是故苾芻當觀段食如子肉想，不應貪著。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、於觸食2　巳一、標當觀</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">隨順樂受觸食因緣，能生於內欲希求苦，是故苾芻當觀順彼六種觸處如無皮牛。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、釋觀相2　午一、舉初觸處2　未一、觀生眾苦</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">應作是觀：若我依於六種觸處，發起種種欲希求貪，便為依止諸色而住；依止色故，令我發起種種諸惡不善尋思。如無皮牛，觸處諸蟲之所唼食，多生眾苦不安隱住。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、結無染住</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是觀已，於初觸處深見過患，無染而住。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7117"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">午二、例餘觸處2　未一、例生苦</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如依於色，如是依聲、香、味、觸、法，當知亦爾。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">未二、例無染</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如於初觸處深見過患，無染而住；如是乃至於第六觸處，當知亦爾。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰三、於意思食2　巳一、出觀苦</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">有漏意會思食因緣，能生於內求不允苦，是故苾芻當觀有漏意會思食如一分火。觀察如是所求不允，能引身心大熱惱故。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">巳二、結住樂</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼作如是正觀察已，終不希望衣食等事，往詣他家，是故不為所求不允所生苦觸，其心坦然安樂而住。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、結</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">由是因緣，應正觀察如是三食，所謂段、觸、意會思食。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、於後識食3　卯一、出觀苦</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7118"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">即由如是三食因緣，生如所說依識內苦，是故苾芻當觀識食如三百鉾之所鑽刺。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、釋所以</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">所以者何？段食因緣，能令非一種種眾多品類病苦依識而起；隨順樂受觸食因緣，能令倍增欲希求苦依識而起；有漏意會思食因緣，能令種種求不允苦依識而起。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯三、結勝利</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是行者，於識食中，正觀諸食以識為依，多生過患。由是因緣，不顧身命。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑三、結能永斷</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">如是如理於四種食審正觀察；審觀為依，能於現法永斷諸食；食永斷故，得至當來後有苦際。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">子五、雜染等2　丑一、喜貪過患2　寅一、略標列</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，若不如實觀此四食，便為喜、貪之所染汙；若為是二所染汙者，當知希求二種過患。一者、當來，二者、現法。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7119"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、隨別釋2　卯一、釋名喜貪</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">於四食中，有漏意會思食因緣，專注希望俱行喜染，名喜。隨順樂受觸食因緣，於能隨順喜樂諸食多生染著，名貪。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》隨順樂受觸食因緣等者：謂由觸食，順生樂受，是名隨順樂受觸食因緣。於六觸處能生喜樂，不見過患，有染而住，是名於能隨順喜樂諸食多生染著。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、釋二過患2　辰一、當來過患</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">此二煩惱，於現法中能染於識，令其安止四種識住，增長當來後有種子；既增長已，生起後有生等眾苦。當知是名喜、貪二種煩惱所作當來過患。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、現法過患</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">彼由如是於四食中安住喜、貪二種煩惱，便於現法有諸塵染；由塵染故，食若變壞，於現法中便生悲歎愁憂萎頓懷慼而住。當知是名喜、貪二種煩惱所作現法過患。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7120"></a><span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">丑二、喜貪依處2　寅一、緣境煩惱依處2　卯一、舉未斷2　辰一、喻入住</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">復次，諸有於此四種食中喜貪未斷，彼六處攝有識之身猶如臺觀六處窗牖，能與緣境煩惱日光作入依處。是光於此或住上地，或住下地。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、顯生苦</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">既得住已，如前所說於四識住能染於識，生起當來後有眾苦。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:#590000"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">《披》是光於此者：謂彼煩惱日光於此識所依處，或住上地，或住下地，能染於識，令於當來受上地生，或下地生。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、翻能斷</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">若有能斷如是喜貪二種煩惱，與彼相違；緣境煩惱尚不得起，況依此入而當得住。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">寅二、煩惱彩畫依處2　卯一、未斷已斷2　辰一、舉未斷</span></strong></span></span><br />
<a name="94p7121"></a><span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">又復若有補特伽羅喜貪未斷；便為魔羅來詣其所，以其種種猶如彩色可愛境界彩畫如是補特伽羅，令其變生種種煩惱相貌顯現。當知如是補特伽羅喜貪未斷，譬如其地，能為種種煩惱彩畫作所依處。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">辰二、翻已斷</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">已斷喜貪補特伽羅，魔詣其所，如前廣說。當知如是補特伽羅喜貪已斷，猶若虛空，非為種種煩惱彩畫作所依處。</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:medium"><span style="color:darkred"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">卯二、過患功德</span></strong></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size:14pt"><span style="color:darkblue"><strong><span style="font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">當知是名於諸食中喜貪未斷如其次第所有過患；當知是名於諸食中喜貪已斷如其次第所有功德。</span></strong></span></span><br />
